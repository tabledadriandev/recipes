# Vegetable glaze (glacé de légumes)

Notes: Use roasted veg stock; finish with a touch of acid.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 90–95; reduce to syrup
Time: 2–4 h
Type: Jus

### Method

1. Reduce strained vegetable stock at a gentle simmer, skimming.
2. Continue until syrupy and coats the back of a spoon.
3. Balance with a drop of vinegar or verjus; strain fine.

### Notes

- Do not scorch. Aim for glossy nappe.
