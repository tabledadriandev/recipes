# Aioli (garlic emulsion)

Notes: Garlic, yolk (or not), mustard, lemon; oil to thick emulsion.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Cold prep
Time: 15–25 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Pound garlic with salt to a paste; whisk in yolk and mustard.

2) Stream in oil to desired thickness; finish with lemon.

3) Adjust with a spoon of water for texture.

### Notes

- For Provençal version, omit egg and work slower; ensure fresh garlic.
