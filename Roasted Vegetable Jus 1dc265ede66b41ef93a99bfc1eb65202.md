# Roasted Vegetable Jus

Cook time min: 90
Cost/portion €: €0.80
Course: Sauce
Ingredients: Carrot 400 g. Onion 300 g. Celery 200 g. Tomato paste 30 g. Water 1.2 L.
Method: Roast 200 °C 45 min. Pressure 40 min. Strain. Reduce to coat.
Prep time min: 20
Status: Approved
Technique: Pressure cook + reduction
Yield g: 900
