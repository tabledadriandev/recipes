# Sauce chasseur (hunter’s)

Notes: Mushrooms, shallot, tomato, white wine; finished with demi and herbs.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Sauté 120–140; reduce 90–95
Time: 30–45 min
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Sauté sliced mushrooms and shallot in butter to light brown.

2) Add tomato concassé; deglaze with white wine; reduce by half.

3) Add demi‑glace; simmer to coat. Finish with tarragon/parsley and butter.

### Notes

- Keep tomato light so mushroom aroma leads.
