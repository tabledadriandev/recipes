# Ceps Confit in Cep Oil (Jar-in-Combi)

## Ingredients

- Ceps (to fill jars)

- Cep oil (to cover to neck)

## Process

1. Fill jars with ceps; cover to neck with cep oil.

2. Cook in combi oven at 130 °C, forced steam, for 1 h 20 min.

3. Cool, refrigerate.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: 130 °C

- **Time**: 1 h 20 min

- **Combi Setting**: Forced steam

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Confit ceps preserved in their own oil.
