# Green vegetable purée (chlorophyll bright)

Notes: Shock hard in ice; blend hot; finish with butter.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 2
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Blanch 98–100; blend 70–80
Time: 20–30 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1. Blanch green veg in salted water until just tender.
2. Shock in ice to set color; drain thoroughly.
3. Blend hot with butter or oil; season; strain.

### Notes

- Reheat gently to avoid dulling color.
