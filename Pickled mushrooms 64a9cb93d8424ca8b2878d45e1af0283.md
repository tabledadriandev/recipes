# Pickled mushrooms

Notes: Quick pickle for garnish; firm texture.
Salt %: 1.5
Shelf Life (days): 7
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Brine 95–100; hold 2–4
Time: 2–48 h
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Simmer brine 3–5% acid, 1.5% salt with aromatics.

2) Pour over blanched mushrooms; cool.

3) Hold 2–48 h.

### Notes

- Keep caps whole for presentation.
