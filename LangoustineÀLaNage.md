# Langoustine à la Nage

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Court bouillon perfumed; langoustines shelled.

- **Cook**: **60–90 s simmer**; rest in nage off heat 2 min.

## Finish

- Chive oil.

- Lemon.

## Parameters

- **Simmer Time**: 60–90 s

- **Rest Time**: 2 min

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Succulent, just-cooked.
