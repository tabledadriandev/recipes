# Vanilla Custard with Burnt Caramel

Cook time min: 40
Course: Dessert
Ingredients: Milk 400 mL. Cream 100 mL. Yolks 100 g. Sugar 100 g. Vanilla. Caramel 50 g sugar + 20 mL water.
Method: Infuse. Temper custard. Caramel to 175 °C. Bake 150 °C 35–40 min.
Prep time min: 20
Status: Approved
Technique: Custard bain-marie
Yield g: 600
