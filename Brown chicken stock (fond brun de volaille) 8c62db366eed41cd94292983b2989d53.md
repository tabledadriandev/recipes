# Brown chicken stock (fond brun de volaille)

Notes: Roast carcasses and mirepoix; deglaze well.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 4
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: 85–92 (simmer)
Time: 4–6 h
Type: Stock

### Method

1. Roast chicken carcasses and mirepoix at 200 °C until well browned.
2. Combine with cold water; bring to a bare simmer and skim.
3. Add tomato paste lightly; simmer 4–6 h; strain.

### Notes

- Clarify for a clean sauce base if needed.
