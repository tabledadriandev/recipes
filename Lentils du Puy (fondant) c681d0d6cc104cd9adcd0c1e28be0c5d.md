# Lentils du Puy (fondant)

Notes: Aromatics, minimal agitation; finish with vinaigrette or butter.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 85–92
Time: 25–35 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Rinse lentils; start cold with mirepoix, garlic, bay, thyme.

2) Simmer gently until just tender; salt at 15 min then finish.

3) Drain; glaze with butter or fold in vinaigrette.

### Notes

- Avoid overcooking to preserve shape.
