# Tomato confit

Notes: Low‑temp olive‑oil roast with aromatics; skin off, seeds optional.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: 90–110 oven
Time: 60–120 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Peel tomatoes (blanch and shock); halve; remove seeds if desired.

2) Toss with olive oil, thyme, garlic, 1.2% salt; arrange snug.

3) Roast low 90–110 °C until collapsed and sweet; cool in oil.

### Notes

- Reserve flavored oil for dressings and glazes.
