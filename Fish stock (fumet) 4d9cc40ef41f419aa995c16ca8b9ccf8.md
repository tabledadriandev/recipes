# Fish stock (fumet)

Notes: Bones and aromatics; never boil; keep clear.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 82–90
Time: 20–35 min
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Sweat mirepoix in butter; add fish bones.

2) Cover with cold water; simmer 20–30 min.

3) Strain gently; chill fast.

### Notes

- Use for sauces, poaching, and velouté.
