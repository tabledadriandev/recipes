# Carrot Tartare, Condiments Cart

## Components

- Hand-chopped compressed carrots

- Condiments: smoked almond, caper-raisin, chive, lemon zest oil, pickled mustard seed, rye crumb.

## Method

- Compress carrot dice with 3% apple juice + 0.3% salt, 2 h.

- Dress Ã  la minute with lemon oil + salt.

## Parameters

- **Compression Brine**: 3% apple juice + 0.3% salt

- **Compression Time**: 2 h

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Cool, glossy, knife-cut texture.

- **Balance**: Sweet/acid/saline.

- **Service**: Serve with warm rye crisps.

## Creative Pairing

The cool, compressed carrot tartare is paired with a tableside condiments cart, allowing the guest to orchestrate the final balance of sweet, acid, and smoke.
