# Panna cotta (vanilla)

Notes: Cream, milk, sugar, gelatin; light set, clean wobble.
Salt %: 0.05
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Bloom gel 5–10; heat 60–70; set 2–4 °C
Time: 4–6 h set
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Bloom gelatin in cold water 5–10 min.

2) Warm cream/milk with sugar and vanilla to 60–70 °C; dissolve gelatin.

3) Portion into molds; chill 4–6 h to set.

### Notes

- Adjust gelatin for desired wobble; avoid boiling dairy.
