# Lemon Verbena Kombucha

## Ingredients

- 1 L filtered water

- 8 g green tea

- 70 g sugar

- 2 g dried lemon verbena

- 100 mL starter kombucha + SCOBY

## Process

1. Brew tea and verbena; steep 5 min.

2. Add sugar, cool to 30 °C.

3. Add starter kombucha + SCOBY.

4. Ferment 7–10 days.

5. Bottle at target pH; refrigerate.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: 26–28 °C (fermentation), 30 °C (inoculation)

- **Time**: 7–10 days

- **pH**: 3.5–4 (bottling target)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: floral, sparkling acid for vinaigrettes or granitas.

- **Stop Cue**: When tart and pH is 3.5–4.

## Creative Application

Use the finished kombucha to create a delicate, floral gelée to accompany a white fish crudo.
