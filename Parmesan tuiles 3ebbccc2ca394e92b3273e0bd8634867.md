# Parmesan tuiles

Notes: Grated parmesan baked into lacy crisps.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–20 °C
Temp °C: Bake 170–180
Time: 6–10 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Spoon grated parmesan thinly on silicone mat.

2) Bake 170–180 °C until golden.

3) Cool and shape while warm if desired.

### Notes

- Season lightly; cheese is salty.
