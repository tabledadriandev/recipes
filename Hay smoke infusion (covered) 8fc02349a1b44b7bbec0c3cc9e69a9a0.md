# Hay smoke infusion (covered)

Applications: Creams, butters, fruits
Confidence: 70
Corrections: Toast hay gently, vent briefly
Failure modes: Acrid smoke, ash
Medium: Air
Notes: Use untreated hay only.
Sensory cue: Sweet hay aroma, no soot
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): Ambient to 80
Time range: 5–20 min
Tools used: Torch, tray, cloche
Type: Smoke

### Method

1) Toast small bundle of clean hay until fragrant. 2) Trap smoke under cloche with target medium (cream, butter, fruit). 3) Infuse 5–20 min; vent if too intense; fine filter.

### Notes

- Sensory: sweet hay, no soot or ash. Ambient to 80 °C depending medium.
- Test: exposure time vs temperature; acceptance = distinct hay note, zero acrid tail.
- Uses: smoked cream, hay butter, fruit aromatization.
