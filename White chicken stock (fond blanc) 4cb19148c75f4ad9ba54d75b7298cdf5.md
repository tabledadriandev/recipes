# White chicken stock (fond blanc)

Notes: Start cold. Skim frequently. No salt. Mirepoix 10–20% of bones by weight.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: 85–92 (simmer)
Time: 3–4 h
Type: Stock

### Method

1. Rinse chicken bones; start in cold water.
2. Bring slowly to a bare simmer; skim diligently.
3. Add mirepoix (10–20%), aromatics; simmer 85–92 °C for 3–4 h.
4. Strain without pressing; chill quickly.

### Notes

- No salt in stock phase.
- Keep below boil to preserve clarity.
