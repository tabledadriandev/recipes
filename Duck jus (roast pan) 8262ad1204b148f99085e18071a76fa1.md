# Duck jus (roast pan)

Notes: Roast carcasses and wings; deglaze pan; reduce with aromatics to nappe.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 4
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Roast 200; deglaze; reduce 90–95
Time: 60–90 min
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Roast duck bones/wings with mirepoix to deep brown.

2) Deglaze trays with water/wine; add to pot with aromatics; simmer 45–60 min.

3) Strain; reduce to nappe; finish with a dot of vinegar and butter.

### Notes

- Brush on sliced breast or use as plate jus.
