# Blackcurrant Oil

## Ingredients

- 300 g blackcurrants (fresh or frozen)

- 200 g grapeseed oil

## Process

1. Heat oil and berries together to 60 °C.

2. Blend until uniform; rest 1 h at 55–60 °C.

3. Strain through fine cloth; chill.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: 60 °C (initial heat), 55–60 °C (rest)

- **Time**: 1 h (rest)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: viscous crimson oil, floral acidity with berry tannins.

## Creative Application

Drizzle over lacto-fermented beets, citrus consommés, or pea miso vinaigrettes for a floral, acidic finish.
