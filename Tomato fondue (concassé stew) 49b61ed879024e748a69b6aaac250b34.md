# Tomato fondue (concassé stew)

Notes: Slow stew of concassé tomatoes with shallot and herbs; base/garnish.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 4
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Sauté 120–140; simmer 90–95
Time: 30–60 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Sweat shallot and garlic in olive oil without color.

2) Add tomato concassé, thyme, bay; simmer until jammy.

3) Finish with basil and olive oil; season.

### Notes

- Use as base for fish, eggs, or pasta; strain for a smoother coulis.
