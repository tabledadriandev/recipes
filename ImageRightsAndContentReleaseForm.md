## Release Terms

1. **Grant of Rights**: The undersigned grants Table d’Adrian and Account the irrevocable, perpetual, and worldwide right to use, reproduce, publish, and distribute any photographs, videos, or content taken during the specified Event/Shoot for marketing, portfolio, website, and social media purposes.

2. **Waiver of Compensation**: The undersigned waives any right to inspect or approve the finished product and waives any claim for compensation arising from the use of the images.

3. **Discretion**: Table d’Adrian agrees to exercise discretion and will not publish images that are overtly intrusive or compromise client privacy without explicit, separate written consent.
