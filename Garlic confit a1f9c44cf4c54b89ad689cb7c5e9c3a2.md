# Garlic confit

Notes: Cloves poached in oil until tender; store submerged.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 10
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Confit 90–100
Time: 60–90 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Cover peeled cloves with oil; add herbs.

2) Poach 90–100 °C until tender.

3) Cool submerged; refrigerate.

### Notes

- Use oil for dressings and sautés.
