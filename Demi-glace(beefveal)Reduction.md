# Demi-Glace (Beef/Veal) Reduction

## Technique

Roast bones + mirepoix → pressure cook 2–3 h with tomato paste and aromatics → strain → reduce to light syrup (glace) → clarify with egg whites or agar freeze-thaw → mount with cold butter at pass.

## Parameters

- **Pressure Cook Time**: 2–3 h

- **Reduction**: Reduce to light syrup (glace).

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Highly concentrated, clarified, and glossy sauce base.
