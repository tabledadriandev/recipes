# Beurre monté base

Notes: Stabilize with small lecithin if needed.
Salt %: 0.8
Shelf Life (days): 2
Storage: Warm hold or rapid chill
Temp °C: 58–65
Time: Hold 5–15 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1. Bring 50–100 mL water or light reduction to a simmer.
2. Off heat, whisk in cold butter cubes gradually to form a glossy emulsion.
3. Maintain 58–65 °C on low heat or bain‑marie, whisking gently.
4. Season and use immediately or hold briefly.

### Notes

- If thinning occurs, whisk in a few drops of cold water.
- Acid splash stabilizes slightly; avoid boiling.
