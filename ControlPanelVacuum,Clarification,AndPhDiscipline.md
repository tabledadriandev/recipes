# Control Panel: Vacuum, Clarification, and pH Discipline

## Core Methods

- **Lacto Acceleration**: Vacuum 100% at 28 °C speeds fermentation. Freeze to halt.

- **Juice Clarification**: Freeze-thaw method, then hang in cheesecloth; **do not press solids**.

- **Kombucha**: Target pH ≈ 3.5; backslop with finished kombucha; freeze to stop.

- **Preservation Safety**: Acidify preservation liquids to **pH ≈ 4.3** before heat-processing.

- **Infused Oils**: Blend-heat to **\~80 °C**, rest overnight, fine-strain, then **freeze** to slow oxidation.

## Parameters

- **Lacto Temp**: 28 °C

- **Kombucha pH**: ≈ 3.5

- **Preservation pH**: ≈ 4.3

- **Oil Temp**: \~80 °C
