# Duxelles (fine)

Notes: Finely chopped mushrooms cooked dry with shallot, thyme; moisture driven off.
Salt %: 1.5
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Sauté 120–140; dry 90–100
Time: 30–45 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Chop mushrooms finely or pulse; sweat dry in a wide pan to drive off moisture.

2) Add butter, shallot, thyme; cook until paste‑like and aromatic.

3) Season; deglaze with a spoon of madeira if desired; reduce dry.

### Notes

- Use as filling, crust binder, or garnish; store airtight.
