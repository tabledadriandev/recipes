# Interview Questions for Hiring Kitchen Staff

## Purpose

Ensures candidates meet the high standards of technical precision and professionalism required by Table d’Adrian.

## Questions

1. **Technical**: Describe your process for achieving a perfect mi-cuit salmon (45°C core). What are the critical control points?

2. **HACCP**: How do you cool a 10L batch of hot stock to 10°C safely? What is the time limit?

3. **Mise en Place**: How do you prioritize a prep list with 10 items and a service starting in 3 hours?

4. **Quality**: Describe a time a sauce split on you. How did you re-emulsify it at the pass?

5. **Philosophy**: What does "flavor clarity" mean to you in a finished dish?

6. **Teamwork**: Describe a conflict with a server regarding plating timing. How did you resolve it?
