# Árbol Chile Oil

## Ingredients

- 100 g árbol chiles

- 1 kg neutral oil

## Process

1. Use the same thermal-blend protocol as Ancho Chile Oil: blend 1 min, heat to 80 °C for 9 min.

2. Fridge overnight.

3. Fine-strain; freeze to slow oxidation.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: 80 °C

- **Time**: 9 min (heat), 1 min (blend)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Spicy chile oil.
