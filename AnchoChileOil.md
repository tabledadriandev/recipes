# Ancho Chile Oil

## Ingredients

- 200 g ancho chiles

- 1 kg grapeseed oil

## Process

1. Blend chiles and oil for 1 min.

2. Heat to 80 °C for 9 min.

3. Fridge overnight.

4. Fine-strain; freeze to slow oxidation.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: 80 °C

- **Time**: 9 min (heat), 1 min (blend)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Deeply flavored chile oil.
