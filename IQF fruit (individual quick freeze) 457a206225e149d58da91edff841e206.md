# IQF fruit (individual quick freeze)

Applications: Desserts, garnishes
Confidence: 75
Corrections: Single layer, pre-chill, airtight bags
Failure modes: Clump, ice burn
Medium: Air
Notes: Use syrup dip to protect if needed.
Sensory cue: Free-flowing pieces, minimal drip
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): -30 blast; hold -18
Time range: 1–3 h
Tools used: Blast freezer, trays
Type: Freeze

### Method

1) Arrange fruit pieces in single layer; pre‑chill. 2) Blast‑freeze at −30 °C until hard. 3) Bag airtight and hold at −18 °C.

### Notes

- Sensory: free‑flowing, minimal drip on thaw.
- Test: layer thickness and pre‑chill; acceptance = no clumping, no ice burn after 48 h.
- Uses: desserts, garnishes.
