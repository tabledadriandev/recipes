# Mushroom duxelles

Notes: Fine chop; cook dry to paste.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Sauté 120–140
Time: 20–30 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1. Finely chop mushrooms and shallots; sweat in butter.
2. Cook off moisture until paste-like; season.
3. Chill; use for tarts, pâté en croûte, sauces.

### Notes

- Deglaze lightly with madeira if desired.
