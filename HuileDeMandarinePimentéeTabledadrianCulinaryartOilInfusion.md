# Huile de Mandarine Pimentée #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Oil #Infusion

**Preface:** A vibrant, aromatic oil, utilizing precise temperature control to infuse the bright citrus of mandarin with the disciplined heat of chili.• 40 g mandarin peel

**Ingredients**

- **Base:**

  - 200 g extra virgin olive oil (Ligurian)

  - 40 g mandarin peel (zest only)

  - 2 g hot chili pepper (finely chopped)

**Method**

1. Peel the mandarins, ensuring all white pith is removed. Coarsely chop the peel and chili pepper.

2. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan to precisely 78°C.

3. Combine the chopped peel and chili with the hot oil. Blend for 5 minutes.

4. Filter the oil through a fine chinois.

5. Transfer the oil to a piping bag and allow it to rest in the refrigerator to separate the purée sediment.

6. Cut the tip of the bag just above the purée layer and discard the residue. Store the clarified oil in an airtight container.

**Technical Notes:** The precise infusion temperature of 78°C is critical for extracting the aromatic compounds without damaging the oil's structure.

**Chef’s Intention:** A disciplined, aromatic accent that provides a controlled heat and citrus lift to a savoury plate.
