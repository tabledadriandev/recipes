# Light Pickles for Brightness

## Technique

Use a brine of 3% salt + 3% sugar + 2% vinegar → pickle vegetables for 12–24 h cold.

## Parameters

- **Salt %**: 3%

- **Sugar %**: 3%

- **Vinegar %**: 2%

- **Time**: 12–24 h

- **Temperature**: Cold
