# Rösti (starch control)

Applications: Garnish, base
Confidence: 75
Corrections: Rinse to control starch, press firmly, moderate fat
Failure modes: Falls apart, soggy
Medium: Fat
Notes: Par-cook or raw per texture.
Sensory cue: Even golden crust, tender center
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): Pan 170–190
Time range: 10–18 min
Tools used: Skillet, spatula
Type: Grill

### Method

1) Rinse and grate or julienne potatoes; rinse to control surface starch (optional for desired bind). 2) Squeeze dry; season. 3) Pack into hot 170–190 °C fat; press to bond; cook until golden; flip and finish.

### Notes

- Sensory: even golden crust, tender center; no soggy patches.
- Test: rinse vs no‑rinse; fat level; acceptance = cohesive slice, crisp both sides.
- Uses: garnish base, canapés.
