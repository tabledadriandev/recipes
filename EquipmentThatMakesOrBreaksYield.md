# Equipment that Makes or Breaks Yield

## Essential Equipment

- **Combi Oven**: For low-loss roasting, steaming, proofing. Program humidity + temp.

- **Blast Chiller**: Cools hot food to safe zone quickly; essential for HACCP.

- **Digital Probe Thermometers**: Calibrate weekly; use one per station.

- **Sous-Vide Setup**: Chamber vac + circulator + stainless tubs.

- **Weigh Scales**: For exact portioning and yield tests.

- **Infrared Gun**: Check surface temps on pans and holding equipment.
