# Chocolate ganache (glossy)

Notes: 1:1 cream:chocolate for base.
Salt %: 0.2
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Emulsify 35–40
Time: 15–30 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1. Heat cream to simmer; pour over chopped chocolate.
2. Rest 1–2 min; emulsify from center until glossy.
3. Adjust with butter or glucose if needed.

### Notes

- Keep below 45 °C to preserve sheen.
