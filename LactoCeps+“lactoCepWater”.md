# Lacto Ceps + “Lacto Cep Water”

## Ingredients

- 2 kg frozen ceps

- 40 g salt (2%)

## Process

1. Seal ceps and salt at 100% vacuum.

2. Ferment 5 d at 28 °C until yellowed and sour.

3. Strain.

4. Clarify liquid by freeze-thaw hang; reserve mushrooms separately.

## Parameters

- **Salt %**: 2%

- **Temperature**: 28 °C

- **Time**: 5 days

## Sensory Cues

- **Stop Cue**: Yellowed and sour.

- **Result**: Clarified liquid (Lacto Cep Water) for seasoning, and preserved mushrooms.
