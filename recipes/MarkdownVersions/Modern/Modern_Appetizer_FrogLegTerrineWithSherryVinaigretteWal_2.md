# FROG LEG TERRINE WITH SHERRY VINAIGRETTE, WALNUTS, FRISÉE, AND CHERRY TOMATOES

**Course:** Appetizers
**Dietary:** AllDiets
**Season:** AllSeasons

**Prep Time:** None
**Cook Time:** None
**Total Time:** None
**Servings:** Six Servings
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

## Ingredients

- 10 to 12 pound pairs frogs’ legs, about 1
- Two tablespoons unsalted butter
- Six tablespoons dry white wine
- One tablespoon finely minced garlic
- Two tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley
- Four ounces walnuts
- Six tablespoons sherry wine vinegar
- Two tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 6 to 10 cherry tomatoes cherry tomatoes
- Heat 1 tablespoon of the butter in a medium-sized sauté pan or skillet over medium heat. When the butter is hot, add the leg bones and sauté for 6 to 8 minutes or until golden brown.
