## Ingredients

- Fifty g Mirabelle Marinade
- Twenty-Five g Spruce Wood Oil*
- 30 to 35 picked purslane leaves picked purslane leaves
- Seven steamed Venus Clams steamed Venus Clams
- Place 5 to purslane leaves in the bottom of a cleaned Venus clam shell. Place 7 cleaned Venusclams standing upright over the purslane with their yellow tongues sticking out. Stick 4 leaves inbetween each clam. Using a pipette, fill the shell up with the sauce until it is full, juicy, and almost spilling out. It should not need seasoning. The Venus clam is now ready to serve.
