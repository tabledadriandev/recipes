## Method

1. It’s imperative not to let the dough get too hot when rising as yeast cells die at 130° F (55°C ).BRIOCHE DOUGH
2. Storage Up to 2 days in the refrigerator, covered in plastic wrap.
3. FreezingAfter the first rising.
4. Defrost in the refrigerator and let rise again.
5. Brush with egg wash and bake.
6. Storage 24 hours in the refrigerator, covered in plastic wrap.
7. FreezingAfter shaping, freeze uncooked.
8. Defrost in the refrigerator and let rise for 1 hour.
9. Brush with egg wash and bake.
