## Ingredients

- Two ounces cream cheese, softened, or ¼ cup crème fraîche,
- One tablespoon fresh lemon juice
- One tablespoon olive oil
- One teaspoon finely snipped chives

- Place 1 crepe on a work surface and top with a salmon round. Spread about 1 tablespoon of the cream cheese over the salmon. Continue the layering, so that you end with a plain crepe on top of the stack.
