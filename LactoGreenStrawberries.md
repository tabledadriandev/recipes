# Lacto Green Strawberries

## Ingredients

- 2 kg green strawberries

- 40 g salt

## Process

1. Mix strawberries and salt.

2. Ferment 5–8 d at 28 °C.

3. Keep reserved cold when soured and juicing.

## Parameters

- **Salt %**: 2%

- **Temperature**: 28 °C

- **Time**: 5–8 days

## Sensory Cues

- **Stop Cue**: When soured and juicing.
