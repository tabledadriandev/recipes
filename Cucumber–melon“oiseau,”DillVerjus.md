# Cucumber–Melon “Oiseau,” Dill & Verjus

## Components

- Compressed melon balls in verjus

- Shaved cucumber ribbons

- Dill oil

- Iced verjus

## Method

- Compress fruit (Vacuum Compression technique).

- Serve in iced verjus.

- Finish with dill oil.

## Parameters

- **Compression Liquid**: Verjus

- **Service**: Iced verjus

## Plating Cues

- **Flavor**: Crystalline acidity.

- **Texture**: High refreshment.

## Creative Pairing

A highly refreshing, cold course where compressed melon and cucumber are served in iced verjus, with dill oil providing a savory, herbaceous counterpoint to the fruit's sweetness.
