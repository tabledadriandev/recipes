# Pickled cucumber brunoise

Notes: Fine dice; quick cure for garnish.
Salt %: 1.5
Shelf Life (days): 7
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Brine 95–100; hold 2–4
Time: 2–24 h
Type: Gel

### Method

1. Boil brine 3–5% acid, 1.5% salt; cool slightly.
2. Pour over cucumber brunoise; chill.
3. Use within 7 days for best texture.

### Notes

- Add dill and mustard seed for aroma.
