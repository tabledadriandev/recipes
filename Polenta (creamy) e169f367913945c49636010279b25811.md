# Polenta (creamy)

Notes: Low, steady heat; finish with butter and parmesan.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 90–95
Time: 30–45 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Bring seasoned water or light stock to simmer; rain in polenta while whisking.

2) Cook low, stirring, until grains bloom and creamy.

3) Finish with butter and parmesan; adjust salt.

### Notes

- Hold with a splash of hot stock; re‑loosen before service.
