# Croquant de Shiso au Micro-Ondes #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Molecular #Garnish

**Preface:** A light, airy crisp, utilizing a precise batter and microwave technique to achieve a fragile, porous structure and concentrated shiso aroma.• Pinch of salt

**Ingredients**

- **Batter:**

  - 600 g water

  - 300 g egg whites

  - 240 g all-purpose flour

  - 8.4 g lecithin

  - 4.2 g xanthan gum

  - Shiso leaves (finely chopped)

  - Pinch of fine salt

**Method**

1. Combine all ingredients in a high-speed blender. Blend until the batter is completely smooth and homogenous.

2. Strain the batter through a fine chinois.

3. Transfer the strained batter to a siphon. Charge the siphon with 2–3 N2O cartridges.

4. Allow the charged siphon to rest in the refrigerator for 24 hours to fully stabilize the aeration.

5. Dispense the batter onto a silicone mat.

6. Microwave at 800W for 1 minute, then reduce power to 400W for 1 minute, until the crisp is set and airy.

7. Cool rapidly and store dry.

**Technical Notes:** The combination of lecithin and xanthan gum is essential for creating a stable, aerated batter that holds its structure during the rapid microwave cooking process.\
**Chef’s Intention:** A disciplined, ephemeral texture that provides a concentrated aromatic lift to a savoury plate.\
