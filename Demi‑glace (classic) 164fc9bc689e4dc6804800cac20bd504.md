# Demi‑glace (classic)

Notes: Espagnole + brown stock reduced to half; base for many sauces.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 7
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Reduce 90–95
Time: 6–8 h
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Combine equal parts espagnole and brown veal stock.

2) Simmer gently, skimming, until reduced by half to a nappant glaze.

3) Strain through fine chinois; chill quickly; portion.

### Notes

- Use as a parent base; salt only at finish in derivative sauces.
