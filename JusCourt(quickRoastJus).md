# Jus Court (Quick Roast Jus)

## Technique

Use pan fond + shallot + red wine reduced by ⅔ → add brown stock → reduce to nappé consistency → strain → finish with sherry vinegar or verjus.

## Parameters

- **Wine Reduction**: Reduce by ⅔

- **Finish**: Reduce to nappé; finish with sherry vinegar or verjus.

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Quick, glossy, acidic jus.
