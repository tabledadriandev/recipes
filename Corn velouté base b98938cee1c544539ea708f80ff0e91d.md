# Corn velouté base

Notes: Use cobs to boost flavor; strain fine.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 85–95; blend 70–80
Time: 30–45 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1. Sweat onions in butter; add fresh corn and cobs.
2. Cover with light stock; simmer 20–30 min.
3. Remove cobs; blend hot to smooth; strain; finish with cream.

### Notes

- Keep color bright by avoiding boil.
