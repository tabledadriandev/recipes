# Glossary of Fermentation Terms

- **LAB**: Lactic Acid Bacteria (for lacto-fermentation).

- **AAB**: Acetic Acid Bacteria (for vinegar and kombucha).

- **SCOBY**: Symbiotic Culture of Bacteria and Yeast (for kombucha).

- **Koji (_A. oryzae_)**: Mold-grown grain rich in amylases and proteases.

- **Peaso**: Noma’s legume-based miso.

- **Moromi**: Shoyu mash under brine.

- **Garum**: Enzymatic umami sauce from brined proteolysis.

- **Blackening**: Low-temp Maillard “cooking” over weeks.

- **Shio Koji**: Salted koji seasoning paste.

- **Amazake**: Sweet koji drink or base.
