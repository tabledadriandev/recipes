# Kombucha: Thesis & Baseline

**Thesis**: A mixed culture (SCOBY) balances yeast and Acetic Acid Bacteria (AAB); monitor Brix and pH to balance sugar and acid.

**Baseline Playbook**:

1. Brew sweet tea and cool.

2. Inoculate with SCOBY + \~10% mature kombucha.

3. Ferment \~7–10 days warm.

4. Bottle near **pH 3.5–4**; condition cold.

**Safety Rail**: Backslop \~10%; ensure **pH near 3.5–4** before bottling to prevent overpressure.
