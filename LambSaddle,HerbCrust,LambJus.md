# Lamb Saddle, Herb Crust, Lamb Jus

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Debone, tie, dry-brine **1% salt** 12 h.

- **Cook**: Roast to **54–56 °C core**.

- **Rest**: 15 min.

## Crust & Sauce

- **Crust**: Panko + parsley + mint + garlic + anchovy oil; broil 1–2 min.

- **Sauce**: Lamb stock reduction; finish with sherry vinegar.

## Parameters

- **Brine Salt**: 1%

- **Core Temp**: 54–56 °C

- **Rest Time**: 15 min

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Herb crust crackle.
