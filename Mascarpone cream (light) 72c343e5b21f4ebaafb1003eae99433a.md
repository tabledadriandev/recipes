# Mascarpone cream (light)

Notes: Mascarpone folded into lightly whipped cream; sweeten to taste.
Salt %: 0.1
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Whip cold
Time: 10–15 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Whip cream to soft peaks with sugar and pinch salt.

2) Fold in mascarpone until smooth.

3) Chill until service.

### Notes

- Flavor with vanilla, citrus, or coffee.
