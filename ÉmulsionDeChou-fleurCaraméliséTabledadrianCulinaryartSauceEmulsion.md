# Émulsion de Chou-Fleur Caramélisé #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Sauce #Emulsion

**Preface:** A refined, savoury emulsion, utilizing the deep, nutty flavour achieved through the disciplined caramelization of cauliflower.• 5g water

**Ingredients**

- **Base:**

  - 0.5 head cauliflower (finely grated)

  - 250 g unsalted butter

  - 5 g water

**Method**

1. In a heavy-bottomed pan, caramelize the grated cauliflower in the butter over medium-low heat until a deep, golden-brown colour and nutty aroma are achieved.

2. Transfer the mixture to a high-speed blender.

3. Introduce the water and blend until the mixture is completely smooth and emulsified.

4. Strain the resulting emulsion through a fine chinois. Serve warm.

**Technical Notes:** The caramelization process is critical for developing the deep, complex flavour profile of the cauliflower.

**Chef’s Intention:** A disciplined, velvety sauce that provides an unexpected depth of flavour to a vegetable course.
