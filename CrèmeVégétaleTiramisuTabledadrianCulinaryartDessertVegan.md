# Crème Végétale Tiramisu #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Dessert #Vegan

**Preface:** A refined, plant-based cream, achieving the classic Tiramisu richness through a disciplined blend of vegan dairy alternatives and precise whipping technique.• 95 ml plant-based whipping cream

**Ingredients**

- **Cream Base:**

  - 95 ml plant-based whipping cream

  - 60 g vegan cream cheese

  - 20 g powdered sugar

  - 15 g vegan sour cream

  - 0.5 tsp vanilla bean paste

- **Coffee Component:**

  - 100 ml concentrated espresso (chilled)

- **Assembly:**

  - Vegan ladyfingers or sponge

  - Cocoa powder (for dusting)

**Method**

1. In a chilled bowl, combine all the ingredients except the vegan sour cream. Whip until soft peaks are achieved.

2. Gently fold in the vegan sour cream. Continue whipping for 1 minute until firm, stable peaks form. Avoid overmixing.

3. Dip the vegan ladyfingers or sponge component briefly into the concentrated espresso.

4. Layer the coffee-soaked component with the prepared cream in a verrine or petit four mold.

5. Chill for a minimum of 2 hours to allow the structure to set.

6. Finish the surface with a disciplined dusting of cocoa powder prior to service.
