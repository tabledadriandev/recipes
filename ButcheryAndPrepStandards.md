# Butchery and Prep Standards

## Beef

- Denude silver skin.

- Leave intramuscular fat.

- Square ends for even sear.

## Lamb

- French racks.

- Remove fell membrane.

- Tie saddles.

## Pork

- Trim gland nodes.

- Score fat cap in a 5 mm grid.

## Poultry

- Air-dry birds 24â€“48 h for crisp skin.

- Separate wishbone for clean carve.

## Flat Fish

- Use 4-fillet method.

- Pin-bone.

- Portion with bias.

## Round Fish

- Use 2-fillet method.

- Belly trim for tartare.

- Loin for roast.
