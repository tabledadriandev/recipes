# Crème anglaise base

Notes: Egg yolk custard; do not boil.
Salt %: 0.1
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Nap 82–85
Time: 15–20 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1. Heat milk/cream with vanilla to steaming.
2. Temper into yolks and sugar; cook to 82–85 °C nappe.
3. Strain; chill rapidly; hold cold.

### Notes

- Over 85 °C risks curdling.
