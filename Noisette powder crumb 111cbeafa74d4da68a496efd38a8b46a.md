# Noisette powder crumb

Notes: Panko tossed in brown butter, toasted to a crisp hazelnut crumb.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 7
Storage: 0–20 °C
Temp °C: Toast 150–170
Time: 10–15 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Toss panko with warm brown butter; season lightly with salt.

2) Toast at 150–170 °C until golden and crisp; cool.

3) Store airtight; re‑crisp before service if needed.

### Notes

- Add lemon zest or herbs to match plate; avoid over‑fatting to keep crisp.
