# Cold infusion oil (herb)

Applications: Finishing oils, emulsions
Confidence: 80
Corrections: Work cold, add antioxidant, purge air
Failure modes: Oxidation, dull color
Medium: Fat
Notes: Spin chlorophyll if needed.
Sensory cue: Bright green, fresh aroma
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 2–4
Time range: 6–24 h
Tools used: Blender, sieve
Type: Infusion

### Method

1) Blend herbs with cold oil; strain fine. 2) Hold at 2–4 °C for 6–24 h to infuse. 3) Optionally spin chlorophyll and fold in for color.

### Notes

- Sensory: bright green, fresh aroma; no oxidation. Work cold; purge air; add antioxidant if needed.
- Test: time 6 vs 12 vs 24 h; acceptance = vivid color, clean aroma.
- Uses: finishing oils, emulsions.
