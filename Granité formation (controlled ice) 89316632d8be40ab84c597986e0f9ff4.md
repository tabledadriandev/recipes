# Granité formation (controlled ice)

Applications: Palate cleansers, desserts
Confidence: 75
Corrections: Stir scrape every 30–60 min, increase sugar
Failure modes: Hard block, large crystals
Medium: Water
Notes: Sugar 15–22% for fine crystals.
Sensory cue: Fine crystals, fluffy texture
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): -18 set; -10 serve
Time range: 2–6 h
Tools used: Freezer, fork
Type: Freeze
