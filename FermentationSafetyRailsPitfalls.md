# Fermentation Safety Rails & Pitfalls

**Default Safety Rails**:

- **Lacto**: 2% salt by weight; submerge; vent CO₂.

- **Kombucha**: pH near 3.5–4 before bottling.

- **Garum**: \~12% salt; \~60 °C accelerating method.

- **Koji**: \~30 °C, 70–75% RH; keep under \~42 °C.

**Pitfalls / Misconceptions**:

- **Botulism fear** is overstated in acidic ferments; keep **pH <4.6** and salt appropriate.

- **Oxygen mistakes**: Lacto needs exclusion; vinegar needs aeration.

- **Temperature drift** collapses koji or over-speeds kombucha.

- **Under-salting** invites spoilage; **over-salting** stalls enzymes.

- **Dirty tools** seed unwanted molds; sanitize all equipment.

- **Bottling kombucha too sweet** risks overpressure.
