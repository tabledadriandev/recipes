# Glace mounting (cold butter finish)

Applications: Pan sauces, jus, glazes
Confidence: 90
Corrections: Cool pan, add splash water, whisk
Failure modes: Greasy, split
Medium: Fat
Notes: Work off heat to stabilize.
Sensory cue: Sheen, nappe, no break
Source: EMP
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): Off-heat 55–65
Time range: 1–3 min
Tools used: Whisk, saucepan
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Reduce sauce to just shy of nappe. 2) Off heat (55–65 °C), whisk in 3–8% cold butter. 3) Check nappe and shine; adjust with a splash of water if too tight.

### Notes

- Sensory: mirror sheen, no oil bleed over 10 min.
- Test: butter % vs pan temp; acceptance = stable nappe for service window.
- Uses: pan sauces, veg glazes.
