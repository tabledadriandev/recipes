# Pommes purée (mousseline)

Notes: Ratios ~2:1 potato:milk by weight; finish with butter 20–30% + cream as needed.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 90–95; finish 60–70
Time: 45–60 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Cook peeled potatoes in salted water from cold to tender; steam‑dry.

2) Pass through tamis/ricer.

3) Heat milk; fold into potato. Mount with cold butter and a splash of cream; season.

### Notes

- Avoid over‑working to prevent gluey texture; hold warm, not hot.
