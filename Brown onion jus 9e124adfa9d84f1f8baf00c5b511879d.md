# Brown onion jus

Notes: Deeply caramelize onions before wetting.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 90–95
Time: 2–3 h
Type: Jus

### Method

1. Slice onions; caramelize slowly to deep brown.
2. Deglaze with wine; add light stock; simmer 2–3 h.
3. Strain fine; reduce to nappe; season after reduction.

### Notes

- Avoid burning onions to prevent bitterness.
