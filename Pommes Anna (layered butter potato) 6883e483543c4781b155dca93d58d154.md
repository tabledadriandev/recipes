# Pommes Anna (layered butter potato)

Notes: Thinly sliced potatoes layered with clarified butter; crisp edge, tender core.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 2
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Bake 180–200
Time: 45–60 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Slice potatoes thin; rinse and dry.

2) Layer in buttered pan, seasoning lightly each layer; press.

3) Bake 180–200 °C until deep golden; weigh while cooling; unmold and portion.

### Notes

- Use clarified butter to avoid scorching milk solids.
