# Béarnaise (estragon)

Notes: Hollandaise derivative with tarragon reduction.
Salt %: 0.8
Shelf Life (days): 0
Storage: Hold warm
Temp °C: Hold 57–60
Time: 10–20 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Make reduction: white wine, vinegar, shallot, tarragon stems → syrup.

2) Whisk yolks over bain‑marie to ribbon; stream in warm clarified butter.

3) Fold in chopped tarragon and reduction to taste; hold 57–60 °C.

### Notes

- Strain reduction to avoid texture; refresh with few drops warm water if thickening.
