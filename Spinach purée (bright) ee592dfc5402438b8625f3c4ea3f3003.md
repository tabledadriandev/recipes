# Spinach purée (bright)

Notes: Blanch‑shock spinaches; blend hot with butter and a little cream; strain.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Blanch 98–100; blend 80
Time: 20–30 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Blanch spinach 20–40 s in salted water; shock in ice; squeeze dry.

2) Blend hot with butter and splash of cream; adjust salt.

3) Pass through fine sieve.

### Notes

- Add a pinch of bicarbonate to blanch water to boost green (optional).
