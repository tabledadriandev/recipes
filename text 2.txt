Oil powder 

4. Basic Oil Powder (Without Maltodextrin)

(Simple cornstarch method)

Ingredients:
	•	30 g flavored oil (e.g., chlorophyll oil, carrot oil)
	•	10–15 g cornstarch

Method:
	1.	In a bowl, slowly drizzle oil into cornstarch while stirring.
	2.	Adjust powderiness by adding tiny extra amounts of cornstarch if needed.
	3.	Sift lightly to remove lumps.

Substitutions:
	•	Any infused oil (basil, chili, parsley, carrot, tomato) — same ratio.
