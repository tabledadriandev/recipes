# Kombu dashi

Notes: Do not boil. Remove kombu before simmer. Base for clarified juice blends.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: 60–70 extract or 20–24 cold steep
Time: 45–60 min hot or 8–12 h cold
Type: Stock

### Method

1. Rinse kombu; soak in water at 60–70 °C for 45–60 min or cold steep 8–12 h at 20–24 °C.
2. Remove kombu before simmering; do not boil.
3. Strain; chill quickly.

### Notes

- Use as a base for delicate broths and clarified blends.
