# Rice pilaf (butter)

Notes: Sweat rice in butter, add hot stock, bake covered; grains separate.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Bake 170–180
Time: 25–35 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Rinse rice briefly; sweat in butter with a pinch of salt.

2) Add hot stock (1:1.5–1.7 rice:liquid); bring to a simmer.

3) Cover and bake 170–180 °C until tender; rest covered; fluff.

### Notes

- Finish with a knob of butter; avoid stirring during cook.
