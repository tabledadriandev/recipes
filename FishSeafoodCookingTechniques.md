# Fish & Seafood Cooking Techniques

## Doneness Goal

- Just-set flakes, moist center.

- **Core Temp**: 50–55 °C = medium; 60 °C = well.

## Cooking Methods

- **Fat Fish (Salmon, Tuna)**: Grill, roast, or pan-sear.

- **Lean Fish (Sole, Cod)**: Poach or steam.

## Poaching

- **Court-Bouillon**: Simmer 10–15 min before adding fish.

## Storage

- Hold on ice at **–1 to 0 °C**; change ice daily.
