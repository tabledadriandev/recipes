# Mayonnaise (cold emulsion)

Applications: Sauces, dressings, aioli base
Confidence: 90
Corrections: Add water dropwise, adjust acid-salt, re‑emulsify with yolk
Failure modes: Split, too thick, dull flavor
Medium: Fat
Notes: Oil 60–80%. Water 10–20%. Acid 0.2–0.6%.
Sensory cue: Thick, glossy, no oil weeping
Source: EMP
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 18–22
Time range: 5–10 min
Tools used: Bowl, whisk or immersion blender
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Whisk yolk, salt, mustard, acid. 2) Stream in oil to 60–80% while whisking until thick and glossy. 3) Adjust with water for texture and acid for balance. Hold 18–22 °C.

### Notes

- Ratios: oil 60–80%, water 10–20%, acid 0.2–0.6%, salt 0.8–1.2%.
- Sensory: no oil weeping after 10 min at room temp.
- Test: oil 60 vs 70 vs 80% and check 30‑min stability. Acceptance: no split at 70% after 30 min.
- Uses: asparagus tips, roast chicken glaze, aioli for poached cod, citrus mayo glaze for cake.
