# Clams, White Wine, Garlic Confit, Parsley

## Prep & Cook

- **Cook**: Sweat garlic confit; add clams + wine; lid **2–3 min**.

## Finish

- Parsley.

- Lemon.

- Grilled bread.

## Parameters

- **Cook Time**: 2–3 min
