# Brown butter milk solids separation

Applications: Clarified noisette, crumbs
Confidence: 85
Corrections: Lower heat, constant monitoring
Failure modes: Burnt solids
Medium: Fat
Notes: Strain immediately for clean fat.
Sensory cue: Amber butterfat, browned solids separate
Source: EMP
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 145–160
Time range: 5–10 min
Tools used: Saucepan, filter
Type: Clarify

### Method

1) Cook butter to hazelnut stage 145–160 °C. 2) Immediately strain through fine filter to separate browned solids. 3) Chill fat if storing; reserve solids for crumbs.

### Notes

- Sensory: clear amber fat, clean hazelnut aroma.
- Test: peak temp and filtration speed; acceptance = zero visible solids, no burnt notes.
- Uses: noisette fat for crumbs and sauces.
