# Basic vinaigrette 3:1

Notes: Oil:acid 3:1 baseline; adjust to taste.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Room (18–22)
Time: 5 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1. Whisk acid, salt, and aromatics until dissolved.
2. Stream in oil while whisking to a light emulsion.
3. Adjust with a splash of water for lighter body.

### Notes

- Add 0.3–0.5% mustard for stability.
