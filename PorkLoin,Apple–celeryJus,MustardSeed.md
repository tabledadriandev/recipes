# Pork Loin, Apple–Celery Jus, Mustard Seed

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: 1% salt + 0.5% sugar, 12 h.

- **Cook**: Roast to **60 °C core**.

- **Rest**: 10 min.

## Sauce & Finish

- **Sauce**: Apple juice + celery leaf + light jus; mount with butter.

- **Finish**: Pickled mustard seeds.

## Parameters

- **Brine Salt/Sugar**: 1% / 0.5%

- **Core Temp**: 60 °C

- **Rest Time**: 10 min

## Plating Cues

- **Flavor**: Sweet-acidic jus.
