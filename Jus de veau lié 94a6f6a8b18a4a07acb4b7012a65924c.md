# Jus de veau lié

Notes: Lightly thickened veal jus; uses blond roux or arrowroot for sheen.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Reduce 90–95
Time: 2–4 h
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Prepare light brown veal stock; clarify if needed.

2) Reduce to near nappe; whisk in small blond roux or arrowroot slurry to light coat.

3) Simmer 5–10 min; strain fine; finish with butter.

### Notes

- Keep viscosity light; avoid gummy texture.
