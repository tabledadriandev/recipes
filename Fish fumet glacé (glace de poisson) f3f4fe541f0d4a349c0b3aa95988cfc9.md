# Fish fumet glacé (glace de poisson)

Notes: Reduced fumet to syrupy glaze; use sparingly.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 82–90; reduce 90–95
Time: 2–3 h
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Prepare clear fish fumet (no boil).

2) Reduce gently to syrupy nappe, skimming.

3) Strain fine; cool fast; portion.

### Notes

- Intensifier for fish sauces; a little goes a long way.
