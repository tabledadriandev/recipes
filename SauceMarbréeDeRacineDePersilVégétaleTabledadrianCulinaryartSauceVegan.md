# Sauce Marbrée de Racine de Persil Végétale #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Sauce #Vegan

**Preface:** A rich, plant-based sauce, achieving a velvety texture and earthy depth, finished with a disciplined marbling of bright parsley oil.

**Ingredients**

- **Sauce Base:**

  - 300 g parsley root

  - 1 L vegetable broth

  - 100 g roasted garlic

  - 90 g blond miso paste

  - 70 g shallot

  - 70 g cashews (hydrated overnight)

  - 40 g lemon juice

  - 30 g olive oil

  - 8 g fine salt

  - 3 g green pepper powder

  - 3 sprigs lemon thyme

  - 1.5 g xanthan gum

- **Garnish:** Parsley oil (for marbling)

**Method**

1. Sauté the shallot and parsley root in olive oil with lemon thyme until softened.

2. Combine the sautéed vegetables, vegetable broth, miso paste, roasted garlic, and hydrated cashews. Simmer gently for 20 minutes.

3. Transfer the mixture to a high-speed blender. Blend until perfectly smooth.

4. Introduce the lemon juice, salt, green pepper powder, and xanthan gum. Blend for 1 minute to stabilize the sauce.

5. Strain the sauce through a fine chinois.

6. To finish, marble the sauce with a disciplined drizzle of bright parsley oil just before service.

**Technical Notes:** The combination of hydrated cashews and xanthan gum provides the necessary fat and stabilization for a rich, dairy-free velouté texture.\
**Chef’s Intention:** A sophisticated, plant-forward sauce that provides both textural richness and a vibrant visual contrast.\
