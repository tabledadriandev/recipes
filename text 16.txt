Gluten-Free Tahini Crackers with Rice Flour

Ingredients (for about 25 crackers):
	•	100 g rice flour
	•	30 g tahini (well-stirred, runny)
	•	1 tbsp olive oil
	•	1/2 tsp salt
	•	1/4 tsp baking powder (optional for slight puffiness)
	•	3–5 tbsp water (start with 3 and add as needed)
	•	Optional: sesame seeds, za’atar, cumin, rosemary, or nigella seeds for topping
