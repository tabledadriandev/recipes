# Nut Praline Crème

## Technique

Caramelize 1:1 sugar:water to deep amber → quench with warm nut milk + salt → blend with 10–12% nut butter until shiny.

## Parameters

- **Caramel Ratio**: 1 part sugar : 1 part water

- **Nut Butter %**: 10–12%

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Shiny, creamy praline texture.
