# Blackening: Thesis & Baseline (Black Garlic)

**Thesis**: Slow Maillard cooking creates sweet, balsamic depth without microbes; this is _not_ a microbial fermentation.

**Baseline Playbook (Black Garlic)**:

1. Place whole garlic heads in a stable chamber.

2. Hold at **\~60 °C** for **6–8 weeks**.

3. Rest at room temp; store chilled.

**Safety Rail**: Maintain **\~60 °C** for 4–8 weeks with stable, low airflow.
