# Brioche croutons (butter)

Notes: Butter‑tossed brioche cubes toasted crisp.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–20 °C
Temp °C: Toast 150–160
Time: 12–20 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Toss brioche cubes with melted butter and salt.

2) Bake 150–160 °C to golden.

3) Cool on rack; store airtight.

### Notes

- Add garlic powder or parmesan for variation.
