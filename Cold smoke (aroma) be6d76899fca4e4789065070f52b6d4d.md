# Cold smoke (aroma)

Applications: Butter, fish, oil
Confidence: 75
Corrections: Clean smoke path, reduce dwell
Failure modes: Soot, condensation
Medium: Air
Notes: Prefer fruitwood.
Sensory cue: Clean smoke aroma, no acrid bite
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 15–25
Time range: 30–120 min
Tools used: Smoker, cold smoke generator
Type: Smoke

### Method

1) Generate clean cold smoke (15–25 °C); route to chamber. 2) Expose product 30–120 min; avoid condensation; vent and rest.

### Notes

- Sensory: clean smoke aroma; no acrid bite. Keep path clean and dry.
- Test: exposure time; acceptance = balanced aroma without soot.
- Uses: butter, fish, oils.
