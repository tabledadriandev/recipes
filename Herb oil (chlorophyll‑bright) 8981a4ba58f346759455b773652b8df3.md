# Herb oil (chlorophyll‑bright)

Notes: Blend blanched herbs with warm oil; spin chlorophyll for vivid green.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 7
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Blanch 98–100; blend 70–80
Time: 30–60 min
Type: Oil

### Method

1) Blanch herbs (parsley, chive) 10–20 s; shock hard; dry fully.

2) Blend with warm neutral oil 70–80 °C until steaming; strain fine.

3) Spin chlorophyll separately if desired and fold for color.

### Notes

- Work cold/fast post‑blend to avoid dulling; store airtight.
