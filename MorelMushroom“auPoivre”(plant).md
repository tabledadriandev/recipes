# Morel Mushroom “Au Poivre” (Plant)

## Components

- Grilled morels

- Green peppercorn–mushroom cream (cashew)

- Brandy reduction

- Chive

## Method

- Char morels.

- Reduce brandy + vegetable jus.

- Mount with cashew cream.

- Crack pepper at the pass.

## Parameters

- **Finish**: Pepper cracked at pass.

## Plating Cues

- **Finish**: Glossy peppered glaze.

- **Aroma**: High perfume (from brandy reduction and cracked pepper).

## Creative Pairing

A plant-based take on a classic French sauce, using cashew cream for richness and fresh cracked green peppercorns for volatile aroma and heat.
