# Dry Aged Duck Breast with Honey Vinegar Glaze

Cook time min: 20
Core temp °C: 54
Course: Meat
Ingredients: Duck breast 2×200 g. Honey 40 g. Red wine vinegar 20 mL. Duck stock 100 mL.
Method: Dry-age 24 h. Render skin. Roast 180 °C to 54 °C. Pan deglaze glaze. Brush.
Prep time min: 15
Status: Approved
Technique: Dry-age + roast + glaze
Yield g: 400
