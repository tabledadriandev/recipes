# Beurre blanc (mount)

Applications: Fish, veg
Confidence: 85
Corrections: Whisk in cold cubes, adjust with cream splash
Failure modes: Breaks on heat, oversour
Medium: Fat
Notes: Shallot reduction to syrup before mounting.
Sensory cue: Satin sheen, no butterfat pooling
Source: EMP
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 55–60 hold
Time range: 10–20 min
Tools used: Saucepan, whisk
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Reduce shallot and acid to light syrup. 2) Off heat, whisk in cold butter cubes gradually to mount. 3) Hold 55–60 °C and season. Do not boil after mounting.

### Notes

- Stability improves with proper reduction. Sensory: satin sheen, no butterfat pooling after 5–10 min warm hold.
- Test: reduction level (light vs syrup) and hold at 58 °C for 10 min; acceptance = stable.
- Uses: leeks, asparagus, turbot, monkfish; citrus variant for fruit.
