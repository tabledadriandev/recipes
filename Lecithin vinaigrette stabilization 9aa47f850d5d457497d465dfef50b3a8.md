# Lecithin vinaigrette stabilization

Applications: Dressings, finishing sauces
Confidence: 75
Corrections: Reduce oil %, add 0.2–0.4% lecithin
Failure modes: Separation, waxy feel
Medium: Fat
Notes: Hydrate lecithin in water phase first.
Sensory cue: Light body, stable film
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 18–22
Time range: 5–10 min
Tools used: Blender
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Hydrate lecithin in water phase. 2) Shear while streaming oil to reach 60–75% oil. 3) Season and adjust viscosity.

### Notes

- Lecithin 0.2–0.4%. Sensory: thin glossy film, no waxiness; stable 30 min.
- Test: lecithin % and shear time; acceptance = no split at room temp.
- Uses: salad dressings, finishing sauces.
