# Koji (A. oryzae): Thesis & Baseline

**Thesis**: _A. oryzae_ grown on steamed grains is the enzyme factory (amylases and proteases) behind the pantry.

**Baseline Playbook**:

1. Soak and steam grain to gelatinize, then cool.

2. Inoculate and incubate at **\~30 °C, 70–75% RH**.

3. Aerate; keep temperature **under \~42 °C**.

4. Harvest **\~48h** when aroma is sweet/nutty; use fresh or dry at \~50 °C.

**Safety Rail**: Maintain **\~30 °C, 70–75% RH**; keep under \~42 °C; harvest \~48h.
