# Lemon posset

Notes: Cream and sugar boiled then set with lemon juice; silky, bright.
Salt %: 0.05
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Boil 100; set 2–4 °C
Time: 4–6 h set
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Boil cream and sugar for 3–5 min.

2) Off heat, whisk in lemon juice and a pinch of salt.

3) Pour into cups; chill 4–6 h.

### Notes

- Zest for aroma; adjust sweetness to balance acidity.
