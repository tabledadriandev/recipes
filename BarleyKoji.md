# Barley Koji

## Ingredients

- 1 kg pearled barley

- 1 g _Aspergillus oryzae_ spores

- water for soaking

## Process

1. Soak barley 6 h; steam 45 min.

2. Cool to 35 °C; inoculate evenly.

3. Incubate 48 h; mix every 12 h.

4. Harvest when ready.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: 30 °C (incubation), 35 °C (inoculation)

- **Humidity**: 70–75 % RH

- **Time**: 48 h (incubation)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: enzymatic base for miso, garum, shoyu.

- **Stop Cue**: When nutty and sweet.

## Creative Application

Use fresh barley koji to cure egg yolks, creating a savory, umami-rich grating element for pasta dishes.
