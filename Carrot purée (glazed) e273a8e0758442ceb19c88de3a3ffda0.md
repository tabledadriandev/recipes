# Carrot purée (glazed)

Notes: Cook carrots in a little stock, butter, and pinch sugar; reduce to glaze before blending.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 90–95; glaze 95
Time: 30–45 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Sweat sliced carrots in butter; add a little stock, sugar, and salt; cover to tender.

2) Uncover and reduce to a shiny glaze.

3) Blend hot; adjust texture with a splash of stock; season.

### Notes

- A drop of citrus brightens; strain for extra silk.
