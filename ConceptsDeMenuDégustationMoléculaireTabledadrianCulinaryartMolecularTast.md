# Concepts de Menu Dégustation Moléculaire #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Molecular #TastingMenu

**Preface:** A framework for advanced plating, utilizing disciplined molecular techniques to create sensory narratives of the Riviera terroir.

**Ingredients**

- **Avant-Garde Dish 1: “The Garden Awakens”**

  - Chlorophyll pearls (from parsley)

  - Beta-carotene foam (from carrots)

  - Chilled pea purée (base)

- **Avant-Garde Dish 2: “The Sea’s Salinity”**

  - Oyster leaf gel

  - Seaweed oil powder

  - Salted lemon air

- **Avant-Garde Dish 3: “The Forest Floor”**

  - Mushroom garum reduction

  - Chestnut purée

  - Truffle oil spheres

**Method**

1. **Chlorophyll Pearls:** Execute agar-based spherification of concentrated chlorophyll extract into a cold oil bath.

2. **Beta-Carotene Foam:** Stabilize beta-carotene oil with lecithin and aerate using a siphon or immersion blender.

3. **Plating:** Apply the chilled pea purée as a disciplined base. Dot the chlorophyll pearls across the surface. Crown the composition with a light, ephemeral beta-carotene foam.

**Technical Notes:** The contrast between the firm, cold spheres and the light, warm foam provides a critical sensory tension.

**Chef’s Intention:** To translate the purity of the Côte d’Azur landscape into a disciplined, multi-sensory experience.
