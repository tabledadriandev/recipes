Applications: Aromatic foams
Confidence: 70

Failure modes: Collapse, large bubbles
Medium: Air
Notes: Use shallow vessel for shear.

Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 2–25
Time range: 5–15 min
Tools used: Hand blender
Type: Emulsion
