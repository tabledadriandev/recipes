# Black Garlic Caramel

## Ingredients

- 100 g black garlic paste

- 100 mL water

- 80 g sugar

- 10 g lemon juice

## Process

1. Cook sugar and water to 120 °C.

2. Stir in black garlic; simmer to syrup.

3. Add lemon juice; strain.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: 120 °C (sugar cook)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: bittersweet, molasses-umami accent for desserts or glaze bases.

## Creative Application

Use the caramel as a bittersweet, savory-umami accent to finish a chocolate dessert or a roasted celeriac dish.
