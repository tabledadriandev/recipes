# Pommes purée (classic)

Notes: Ricer; high butter; finish with hot milk/cream.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 2
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Cook 95–100; mount 60–70
Time: 45–60 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1. Boil peeled potatoes until tender; drain and dry steam.
2. Pass through ricer; fold in warm butter, then hot milk/cream.
3. Season; adjust texture; keep warm.

### Notes

- Do not overwork to avoid gluey texture.
