# Cracker de Café Express #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Cracker #Sweet

**Preface:** A fragile, aromatic crisp, designed for immediate service, offering a concentrated note of roasted coffee.• Butter: 50 g

**Ingredients**

- **Base:**

  - 50 g all-purpose flour

  - 50 g granulated sugar

  - 50 g egg whites

  - 50 g unsalted butter

  - 3 tsp instant coffee powder (finely milled)

**Method**

1. Gently melt the butter.

2. Combine all ingredients to form a smooth, fluid batter.

3. Spread the batter thinly onto a silicone mat or parchment paper.

4. Bake at 170°C until the edges are deep brown and the center is crisp.

5. Remove and cool rapidly.

**Technical Notes:** The high sugar and fat content ensures a rapid caramelization and a brittle texture.

**Chef’s Intention:** A precise, bitter accent to conclude a tasting menu.
