# Olive brine curing (de-bittering)

Applications: Garnishes, oils
Confidence: 60
Corrections: Increase salinity 8–10%, change brine
Failure modes: Softening, surface growth
Medium: Water
Notes: Rinse cycles accelerate de-bittering.
Sensory cue: Reduced bitterness, firm texture
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 18–24
Time range: 2–8 wk
Tools used: Jars, brine, weights
Type: Cure

### Method

1) Soak fresh olives in 8–10% brine at 18–24 °C; change brine regularly. 2) Rinse cycles to accelerate de‑bittering. 3) Finish in seasoned brine; store.

### Notes

- Sensory: reduced bitterness, firm texture; no softening.
- Test: brine % and change frequency; acceptance = pleasant bitterness balance.
- Uses: garnishes, oils.
