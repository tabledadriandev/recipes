# Nitrite-free pink curing (beet-based)

Applications: Fish, roots
Confidence: 60
Corrections: Thinner cuts, inject, extend time
Failure modes: Uneven penetration
Medium: Water
Notes: Use beet juice and salt for color.
Sensory cue: Even color, cured aroma
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 0–4
Time range: 12–72 h
Tools used: Vacuum bag, scale
Type: Cure

### Method

1) Mix beet juice and brine; submerge product under vacuum if possible. 2) Cure at 0–4 °C for 12–72 h depending on thickness. 3) Rinse lightly; dry; rest.

### Notes

- Sensory: even color, cured aroma; monitor penetration.
- Test: thickness vs time; acceptance = uniform cure without mealy texture.
- Uses: fish, roots.
