# Black garlic production

Applications: Sauces, caramels, glazes
Confidence: 70
Corrections: Increase humidity, wrap bulbs
Failure modes: Drying, off-notes
Medium: Air
Notes: Humidity 70–90% recommended.
Sensory cue: Dark, jammy cloves, balsamic aroma
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 60–75
Time range: 14–40 d
Tools used: Fermenter, rice cooker, probe
Type: Fermentation

### Method

1) Hold whole bulbs at 60–75 °C, 70–90% RH for 14–40 days. 2) Wrap to prevent drying; check weekly. 3) Cool; peel; store vacuum‑sealed.

### Notes

- Sensory: dark, jammy cloves; balsamic aroma.
- Test: temp and RH; acceptance = soft, uniform black without hard cores.
- Uses: sauces, caramels, glazes.
