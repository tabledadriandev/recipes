# Pan roasting with butter basting

Applications: Meats, large mushrooms
Confidence: 85
Corrections: Skim solids, lower heat, flip more
Failure modes: Burnt solids, uneven cook
Medium: Fat
Notes: Aromatics thyme, garlic.
Sensory cue: Even crust, aromatic foam
Source: EMP
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): Sear 180–220; baste 60–90
Time range: 6–12 min
Tools used: Skillet, spoon, probe
Type: Grill

### Method

1) Sear in neutral oil at 180–220 °C to crust. 2) Add butter, thyme, garlic; baste in 60–90 °C foam. 3) Rest 5–10 min; slice and serve.

### Notes

- Sensory: mahogany crust, hazelnut butter; no burnt solids.
- Test: vary basting temp and frequency; acceptance = core ±1 °C, butter stays noisette.
- Uses: ribeye, veal chop, king oyster mushrooms.
