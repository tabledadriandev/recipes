# Beef Short Rib, Coffee-Cocoa Glaze

## Prep & Cook

- **Brine**: 1.2% salt, 0.6% sugar, 18 h.

- **Cook**: Braise with onion, stock 90–95 °C, 3–4 h to probe-tender. Chill and portion.

## Glaze & Finish

- **Glaze**: Coffee reduction + demi-glace + glucose to **104 °C**.

- **Finish**: Reheat portions in glaze; serve with celery root purée.

## Parameters

- **Brine Salt/Sugar**: 1.2% / 0.6%

- **Braise Temp**: 90–95 °C

- **Braise Time**: 3–4 h

- **Glaze Temp**: 104 °C

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Probe-tender.

- **Finish**: Lacquered glaze.
