# Lacto Koji Water (Seasoning)

## Ingredients

- 1 kg koji

- 2 L water

- 60 g salt

## Process

1. Blend all ingredients.

2. Seal and ferment 2–3 d at 28 °C.

3. Freeze to hold when target pH is reached.

## Parameters

- **Salt %**: 3% (60g salt / 2kg total weight)

- **Temperature**: 28 °C

- **Time**: 2–3 days

- **pH**: ≤ 4.5 (target)

## Sensory Cues

- **Stop Cue**: Taste should read sweet-sour-fruity (not vinegary).
