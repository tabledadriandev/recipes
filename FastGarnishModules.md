# Fast Garnish Modules

## Pickled Mustard Seeds

- **Brine**: 1:1:1 water:sugar:vinegar + 2% salt.

- **Cook**: Simmer seeds 30–40 min; hold.

## Fermented Green Peppercorns

- **Brine**: 3% brine.

- **Time**: 10–14 d.

## Citrus Oil

- **Prep**: Zest in oil at 60 °C for 1 h; strain.

## Bone-Marrow Oil

- **Prep**: Roast bones; drain fat; filter warm.
