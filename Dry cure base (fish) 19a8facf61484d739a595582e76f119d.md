# Dry cure base (fish)

Notes: Salt 2–3%, sugar 1–2%, citrus zest optional.
Salt %: 2.5
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: 0–3 °C
Time: 2–12 h
Type: Gel

### Method

1. Mix salt and sugar; add aromatics.
2. Coat fish evenly; cure 2–12 h depending on thickness.
3. Rinse lightly; dry; rest uncovered 12 h for pellicle if smoking.

### Notes

- Adjust time to cut thickness.
