# Scallops, Apple–Celery Juice, Caviar (Optional)

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Dry, salt lightly.

- **Cook**: Hard sear **45–60 s per side**.

## Finish

- Chilled apple–celery juice.

- Caviar (optional).

- Chive.

## Parameters

- **Sear Time**: 45–60 s per side

## Plating Cues

- **Contrast**: Hot sear vs. chilled juice.
