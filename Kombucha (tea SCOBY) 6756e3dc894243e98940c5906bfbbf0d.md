# Kombucha (tea SCOBY)

Applications: Acid component, glazes
Confidence: 75
Corrections: Lower temp, shorter ferment, clean tools
Failure modes: Mold growth, vinegar harshness
Medium: Sugar
Notes: Sugar 6–8%, tea to taste.
Sensory cue: Pleasant acidity, light effervescence
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 22–28
Time range: 7–21 d
Tools used: Glass jar, cloth cover
Type: Fermentation

### Method

1) Brew tea; add 6–8% sugar; cool to 22–28 °C. 2) Add SCOBY; ferment 7–21 days to pleasant acidity. 3) Strain; chill or second‑ferment.

### Notes

- Sensory: light effervescence, pleasant acidity; no mold.
- Test: time and tea strength; acceptance = balanced acid without vinegar harshness.
- Uses: acid component, glazes.
