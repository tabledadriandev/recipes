# Clarified apple stock

Notes: Keep covered to prevent oxidation.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: 2–4 or 40–50
Time: 2–24 h
Type: Gel

### Method

1. Treat fresh apple juice with 0.1–0.3% pectinase.
2. Hold at 40–50 °C for 1–2 h, then chill to 2–4 °C.
3. Filter through fine mesh or paper until brilliant clarity.
4. Store covered cold to prevent oxidation.

### Notes

- Add 0.1% ascorbic + 0.05% citric to limit browning.
