# Beurre noisette lemon caper

Notes: Brown butter finished with lemon, capers, parsley; fish & veg finish.
Salt %: 0.8
Shelf Life (days): 0
Storage: Hold warm
Temp °C: Cook 145–160; finish 55–60
Time: 10–15 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Brown butter to hazelnut; remove from heat.

2) Add capers and lemon juice; foam subsides. Stir in chopped parsley.

3) Spoon over fish/veg; season.

### Notes

- Add a touch of chicken glaze for extra body if desired.
