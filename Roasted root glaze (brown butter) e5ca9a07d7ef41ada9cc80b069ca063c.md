# Roasted root glaze (brown butter)

Notes: Finish with brown butter and jus.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 2
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Roast 200; glaze 85–95
Time: 45–60 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1. Roast roots at 200 °C to color.
2. Deglaze tray; add a splash of stock; reduce.
3. Mount with brown butter to coat.

### Notes

- Season after reduction.
