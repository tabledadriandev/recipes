# Chicken glaze (glacé de volaille)

Notes: Reduce white stock to syrupy glaze.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 90–95; reduce
Time: 4–6 h
Type: Jus

### Method

1. Reduce strained white chicken stock at a gentle simmer.
2. Skim often; continue to syrupy nappe.
3. Strain fine; chill; portion.

### Notes

- Use sparingly as seasoning.
