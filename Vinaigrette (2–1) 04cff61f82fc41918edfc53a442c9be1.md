# Vinaigrette (2–1)

Notes: 2 parts oil to 1 acid; mustard, salt, pepper.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 7
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Cold prep
Time: 10–15 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Whisk acid, salt, mustard.

2) Stream in oil to emulsify.

3) Adjust seasoning; add herbs.

### Notes

- Thin with a spoon of water for light coats.
