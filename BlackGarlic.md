# Black Garlic

## Ingredients

- whole garlic heads

## Process

1. Place whole heads in rice cooker or chamber.

2. Maintain temperature for 6–8 weeks.

3. Rest 1 week at room temperature.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: 60 °C

- **Time**: 6–8 weeks (cooking), 1 week (rest)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: sweet, molasses-like paste for sauces and purées.

## Creative Application

Purée the black garlic with a touch of vinegar and oil to create a deeply flavored, dark smear for plating alongside roasted meats or vegetables.
