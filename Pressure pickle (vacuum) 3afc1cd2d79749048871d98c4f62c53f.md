# Pressure pickle (vacuum)

Applications: Fruits, cucumbers, citrus peels
Confidence: 75
Corrections: Lower vacuum %, thicker cuts
Failure modes: Mushy, uneven
Medium: Water
Notes: Use 2–3% salt, 2–4% acid base.
Sensory cue: Translucent, crisp, quick penetration
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 2–4
Time range: 5–60 min
Tools used: Chamber vac
Type: Cure

### Method

1) Combine 2–3% salt, 2–4% acid brine; chill. 2) Vacuum fruits/veg to pressure‑pickle 5–60 min at 2–4 °C. 3) Release vacuum; hold chilled.

### Notes

- Sensory: translucent, crisp; even penetration.
- Test: vacuum % and time; acceptance = firm texture, bright acidity.
- Uses: cucumbers, fruits, citrus peels.
