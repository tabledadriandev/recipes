# Black garlic purée

Notes: Fermented garlic blended with stock and oil to glossy purée.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Blend 60–70
Time: 15–20 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Blend black garlic with warm stock and a little oil.

2) Pass fine; season.

3) Hold cold; rewarm gently.

### Notes

- A drop of vinegar balances sweetness.
