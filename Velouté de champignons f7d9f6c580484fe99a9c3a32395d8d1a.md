# Velouté de champignons

Notes: Mushroom soup finished with cream and butter.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 85–92
Time: 30–45 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Sauté mushrooms and shallot in butter.

2) Add light stock; simmer 20–30 min.

3) Blend; finish with cream/butter; strain and season.

### Notes

- A dash of sherry lifts aroma.
