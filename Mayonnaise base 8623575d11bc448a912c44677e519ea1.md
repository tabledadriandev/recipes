# Mayonnaise base

Notes: Oil 60–80%, yolk 10–15%, water 10–20%, acid 0.2–0.6%.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Room (18–22)
Time: 5–10 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1. Whisk yolk, salt, mustard, acid until homogeneous.
2. Drizzle in oil while whisking to form a stable emulsion.
3. Adjust with water for texture and acid for balance.

### Notes

- Re‑emulsify splits with a fresh yolk starter.
- Keep cold for food safety.
