# Lardons (crisp)

Notes: Slow render to crisp; strain and reserve fat.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Render 140–160
Time: 10–20 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Start lardons cold in pan; render at 140–160 °C.

2) Stir until golden and crisp.

3) Strain; cool on rack.

### Notes

- Save rendered fat for sauté and glazes.
