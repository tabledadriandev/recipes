# Pâte de Tomate et Crumble d'Olive Noire #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Dough #Advanced

**Preface:** An advanced, savoury dough, utilizing concentrated tomato and almond powder to create a robust, richly flavoured base for a refined taco composition.Time required: \~3 hours (including resting)

**Ingredients**

- **Tomato Taco Dough:**

  - 120 g all-purpose flour

  - 60 g tomato paste

  - 50 g almond powder

  - 20 g Ligurian olive oil

  - 15 g egg yolk

  - 15 g unsalted butter

  - 10 g granulated sugar

  - 5 g sriracha sauce

  - 4 g tomato powder

  - 3 g fine salt

- **Black Olive Crumble:**

  - 100 g black olives (Taggiasca)

  - 50 g all-purpose flour

  - 20 g unsalted butter

  - 10 g black olive oil

  - 5 g fine salt

**Method**

1. **Dough:** Combine flour, almond powder, sugar, salt, and tomato powder. Incorporate the egg yolk, olive oil, and sriracha. Mix until a cohesive mass forms. Introduce the softened butter and knead for 5 minutes. Rest the dough for 1 hour.In a bowl, mix flour, almond powder, sugar, salt, tomato powder. Then incorporate the egg yolk, butter (softened), sriracha sauce, olive oil, and tomato paste.

2. **Crumble:** Combine all crumble ingredients. Mix until a coarse, sandy texture is achieved. Bake at 160°C until crisp and dry.Knead the mixture into a dough. Let it rest for 2 hours in the fridge.

3. **Assembly:** Roll the dough thinly. Cut into precise taco shapes. Bake at 180°C until crisp. Fill with the desired component and finish with a disciplined scattering of the black olive crumble.Bake at 160°C for 15 minutes. Let cool and keep dry for later.

**Technical Notes:** The high concentration of tomato solids requires precise hydration control to achieve the correct dough consistency.\
**Chef’s Intention:** A technically demanding base that provides a concentrated, savoury foundation for seasonal fillings.\
