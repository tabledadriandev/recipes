# Croutons (service)

Notes: Uniform diced bread dehydrated for crunch; finish with flavored oil.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 7
Storage: 0–20 °C
Temp °C: Dehydrate 110–130
Time: 20–40 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Dice day‑old bread evenly; toss with a touch of oil and salt.

2) Dry at 110–130 °C until crisp without color.

3) Toss warm with flavored oil (garlic, herb) and cool.

### Notes

- Store airtight with desiccant; re‑crisp briefly if needed.
