# Lacto Baseline (EMP Style)

## Technique

Use 2% salt by weight → ferment at 20–25 °C until tangy (3–7 d) → refrigerate to hold.

## Parameters

- **Salt %**: 2%

- **Temperature**: 20–25 °C

- **Time**: 3–7 days

## Sensory Cues

- **Stop Cue**: When tangy.
