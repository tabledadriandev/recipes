# Brown butter crumb

Notes: Use clarified brown butter.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 7
Storage: 0–20 °C
Temp °C: Toast 150–170
Time: 10–15 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1. Toss panko with brown butter; season.
2. Toast 150–170 °C until crisp; cool.
3. Store airtight; re-crisp briefly if needed.

### Notes

- Add lemon zest for brightness.
