# Port wine reduction

Notes: Port reduced with aromatics to syrup; finish sauces or desserts.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Reduce 90–95
Time: 30–45 min
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Reduce port with orange zest, star anise to syrup.

2) Option: add demi for savory, or butter for gloss.

3) Strain and hold.

### Notes

- Watch bitterness; do not scorch sugars.
