# Lacto Green Gooseberries

## Ingredients

- 1 kg gooseberries

- 20 g salt

## Process

1. Mix gooseberries and salt.

2. Ferment 5 d at 28 °C.

3. Freeze juice and berries to halt fermentation.

## Parameters

- **Salt %**: 2%

- **Temperature**: 28 °C

- **Time**: 5 days

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Tart-salty preserved gooseberries and juice.
