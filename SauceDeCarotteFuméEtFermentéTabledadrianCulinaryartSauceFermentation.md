# Sauce de Carotte Fumé et Fermenté #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Sauce #Fermentation

**Preface:** A complex sauce, combining the bright acidity of fermented carrot juice with the depth of smoked butter and a disciplined emulsification.

**Ingredients**

- **Fermented Juice:**

  - Carrot juice (freshly pressed)

  - 2% fine salt (by weight of juice)

- **Sauce Base:**

  - 75 ml fermented carrot juice

  - 25 ml sushi vinegar

  - 100 g unsalted butter

  - Apple smoke dust (fine)

**Method**

1. **Fermentation:** Juice the carrots. Add 2% salt by weight. Place in a sterilized jar at 21°C. Stir daily for 10 days.

2. **Smoked Butter:** Lightly smoke the butter using apple smoke dust. Clarify and reserve.

3. **Sauce:** Combine the fermented carrot juice and sushi vinegar. Bring to a rapid boil and reduce slightly.

4. Remove from heat. Gradually incorporate the smoked butter using a hand blender to emulsify and mount the sauce (_beurre monté_ technique).

5. Season with salt and serve warm.

**Technical Notes:** The _beurre monté_ technique is essential for achieving a stable, rich emulsion without the use of starch.

**Chef’s Intention:** A disciplined balance of acidity, umami, and smoke that elevates a primary protein.
