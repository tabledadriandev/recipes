# Sweet potato purée

Notes: Roast for caramelized notes; blend with butter and cream.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Roast 180–200; blend 80
Time: 45–70 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Roast whole sweet potatoes until soft.

2) Scoop flesh; blend hot with butter/cream.

3) Strain; season; adjust with stock.

### Notes

- A little miso adds depth.
