# Sauce gribiche

Notes: Cold emulsified sauce with hard‑boiled yolk, mustard, oil, cornichon, caper, herbs.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 2
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Cold prep
Time: 20–30 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Mash hard‑boiled yolks with Dijon and a splash of vinegar.

2) Whisk in neutral oil to a loose emulsion; adjust with a little warm water.

3) Fold in brunoise cornichons, capers, chopped parsley/chervil, and minced whites.

### Notes

- Balance with extra vinegar and a little salt. Serve with fish, vegetables, or meats.
