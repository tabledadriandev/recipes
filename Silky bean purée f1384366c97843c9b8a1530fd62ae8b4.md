# Silky bean purée

Notes: Cook with aromatics; finish with olive oil.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 90–95; blend 80
Time: 60–90 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1. Simmer soaked beans with aromatics until tender.
2. Strain; reserve liquid; blend hot with oil and some liquor.
3. Pass fine; adjust with liquor for silkiness.

### Notes

- Avoid graininess by fully cooking beans.
