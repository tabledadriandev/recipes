# Sauce vin blanc (fish velouté)

Notes: Fish velouté reduced with white wine; finish with cream or butter.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 85–92; finish 55–60
Time: 30–45 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Reduce white wine with shallots to a light syrup.

2) Add fish velouté; simmer to nappant.

3) Finish with cream or butter; strain; adjust lemon and salt.

### Notes

- Keep heat low after enrichment to prevent breaking.
