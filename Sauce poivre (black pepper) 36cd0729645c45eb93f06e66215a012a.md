# Sauce poivre (black pepper)

Notes: Crushed black pepper sauce finished with cream or butter; steak service.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Reduce 90–95
Time: 30–45 min
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Sweat crushed black pepper and shallot in butter.

2) Deglaze with cognac; flambé or reduce by half.

3) Add demi‑glace; simmer to coat. Finish with a splash of cream or butter.

### Notes

- Adjust heat by pepper quantity; season salt only at end.
