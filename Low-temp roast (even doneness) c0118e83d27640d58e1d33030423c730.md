# Low-temp roast (even doneness)

Applications: Beef, venison, poultry crowns
Confidence: 85
Corrections: Sear after, baste, higher final sear
Failure modes: Dry exterior, no crust
Medium: Air
Notes: Rest 5–10 min before carve.
Sensory cue: Uniform edge-to-center doneness
Source: EMP
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 90–130 oven; sear 200+
Time range: 20–120 min
Tools used: Probe, rack, pan
Type: Grill

### Method

1) Roast at 90–130 °C on rack to near‑final core; baste periodically. 2) Rest 5–10 min. 3) Sear at 200+ °C for crust; glaze or mount as needed.

### Notes

- Sensory: uniform edge‑to‑center doneness; crisp exterior after sear.
- Test: roast temp and sear time; acceptance = target core ±1 °C, crust without dryness.
- Uses: beef, venison, poultry crowns.
