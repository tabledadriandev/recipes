# Compound butter — herb

Notes: Soft butter with parsley, chive, lemon zest; roll and chill.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 7
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Cold prep
Time: 15–20 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Cream soft butter with salt.

2) Fold herbs and zest.

3) Roll in film; chill; slice to serve.

### Notes

- Add anchovy or capers for fish service.
