# Foie Gras Torchon, Sauternes Gel (Optional)

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Devein; 2% salt, 0.4% sugar, white pepper, Armagnac.

- **Cook**: Bag, **58 °C for 35–40 min**; ice bath; hang overnight.

## Finish

- Sauternes gel sheet.

- Toast brioche.

## Parameters

- **Cure Salt/Sugar**: 2% / 0.4%

- **SV Temp/Time**: 58 °C / 35–40 min

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Silken, chilled torchon.
