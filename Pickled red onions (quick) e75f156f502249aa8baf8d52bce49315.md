# Pickled red onions (quick)

Notes: Hot brine, thin slices; vivid color with red wine vinegar or hibiscus.
Salt %: 1.5
Shelf Life (days): 10
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Brine 95–100; hold 2–4
Time: 2–24 h
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Boil brine 3–5% acid, 1.5% salt with aromatics.

2) Pour over thinly sliced red onions; cool and chill.

3) Use from 2 h to 10 days.

### Notes

- Keep slices even; add hibiscus for color and floral notes.
