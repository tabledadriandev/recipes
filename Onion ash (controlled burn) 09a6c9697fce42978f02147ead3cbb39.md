# Onion ash (controlled burn)

Applications: Seasoning, oils
Confidence: 75
Corrections: Lower temp, dehydrate before grinding
Failure modes: Acrid, clumpy
Medium: Air
Notes: Blend with maltodextrin to flow.
Sensory cue: Sweet bitter balance, fine powder
Source: Noma
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 200–240 roast; finish torch
Time range: 60–120 min
Tools used: Oven, dehydrator, grinder
Type: Smoke

### Method

1) Roast onions at 200–240 °C to deep char; dehydrate if needed; grind to fine powder. 2) Sieve; blend with maltodextrin for flow; store airtight.

### Notes

- Sensory: sweet‑bitter balance; fine, non‑gritty powder.
- Test: roast temp/time and dehydration; acceptance = no acrid notes, free‑flowing.
- Uses: seasoning, oils, dusts.
