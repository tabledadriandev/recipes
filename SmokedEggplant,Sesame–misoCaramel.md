# Smoked Eggplant, Sesame–Miso Caramel

## Components

- Coal-smoked eggplant

- Sesame-miso caramel

- Lemon vinegar

- Herb salad

## Method

- Burn-skin roast eggplant.

- Fold miso into caramel to silk.

- Glaze, torch lightly.

## Parameters

- **Eggplant Prep**: Burn-skin roast

## Plating Cues

- **Finish**: Lacquered, sweet–salty, bitter edge.

## Creative Pairing

The intense, smoky bitterness of the eggplant is balanced by a rich, silken sesame-miso caramel, with lemon vinegar providing a necessary acidic cut.
