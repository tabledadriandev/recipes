# Brown Butter Notes without Dairy

## Technique

Toast almond or hazelnut oil with milk crumb (oat) for 10 min at 150 °C → strain → use for “noisette” aromatic oil.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: 150 °C

- **Time**: 10 min

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Dairy-free "noisette" aromatic oil with brown butter notes.
