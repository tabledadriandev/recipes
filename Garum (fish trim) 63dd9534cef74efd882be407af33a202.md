# Garum (fish trim)

Applications: Seasoning, sauces, glazes
Confidence: 80
Corrections: Raise salt to 12–18%, ensure 55 °C stability
Failure modes: Rot odor, surface growth
Medium: Water
Notes: Salt 12–18% of total with koji.
Sensory cue: Deep umami, clear amber liquid
Source: Noma
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 50–60
Time range: 30–60 d
Tools used: Incubator, jars
Type: Fermentation

### Method

1) Combine fish trim with koji and 12–18% salt. 2) Incubate 50–60 °C for 30–60 days, stirring periodically. 3) Press and strain; pasteurize if desired.

### Notes

- Sensory: deep umami, clear amber liquid; no rot odors.
- Test: salt % and temperature; acceptance = clean aroma, target salinity.
- Uses: seasoning, sauces, glazes.
