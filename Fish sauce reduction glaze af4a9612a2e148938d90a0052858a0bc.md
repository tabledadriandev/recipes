# Fish sauce reduction glaze

Notes: Noma-style umami glaze with aromatics.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 7
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Reduce 90–95
Time: 15–25 min
Type: Jus

### Method

1. Combine fish sauce with a little sugar and aromatics.
2. Simmer and reduce to a light syrup.
3. Strain; cool; use as brush-on glaze.

### Notes

- Powerful. Apply thinly.
