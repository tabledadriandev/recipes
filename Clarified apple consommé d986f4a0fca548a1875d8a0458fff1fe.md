# Clarified apple consommé

Notes: Pectinase 0.1–0.3%. Fine filter or gelatin clarification variant.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: 2–4 (settle) or 40–50 (enzyme)
Time: 6–12 h
Type: Gel

### Method

1. Treat apple juice with 0.1–0.3% pectinase at 40–50 °C for 1–2 h.
2. Chill to 2–4 °C and hold to settle.
3. Filter very fine or use gelatin clarification variant.

### Notes

- Keep covered and cold to prevent oxidation.
