# Parmesan foam (warm)

Notes: Light espuma from parmesan infusion; ISI or hand blender foam.
Salt %: 0.8
Shelf Life (days): 0
Storage: Hold warm
Temp °C: Hold 55–60
Time: 20–30 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Infuse grated parmesan in hot milk/cream; strain.

2) Season; add a little lecithin or use ISI with 1–2 chargers.

3) Hold 55–60 °C; dispense onto plate.

### Notes

- Do not boil; taste salt carefully (cheese salinity varies).
