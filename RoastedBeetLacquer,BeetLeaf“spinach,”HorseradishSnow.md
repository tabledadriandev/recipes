# Roasted Beet Lacquer, Beet Leaf “Spinach,” Horseradish Snow

## Components

- Beet jus glaze

- Roasted beets

- Wilted beet greens with almond butter monté

- Frozen horseradish almond milk

## Method

- Roast beets in salt.

- Reduce beet juice + glucose to 104 °C.

- Microplane frozen horseradish at the pass.

## Parameters

- **Glaze Temp**: 104 °C (syrup point)

## Plating Cues

- **Finish**: Mirror lacquer.

- **Contrast**: Hot–cold contrast (warm beets vs. frozen snow).

## Creative Pairing

A study in temperature and texture, contrasting the warm, sweet, lacquered beet with the volatile, cold, and sharp horseradish snow.
