Pasta DOUGH USING
DEHYDRATED VEGETABLE
POWDERS


Makes around 150g/5½oz dough, enough for 1, or to use for making patterns

70g/2½oz Italian 00 flour
30g/loz fine semolina
1 tsp powder of your choice
1 egg

Option Two

Makes around 450g/1lb dough, enough for 4

210g/7½oz Italian 00 flour
90g/3¼oz fine semolina
3 tsp powder of your choice
3 eggs
