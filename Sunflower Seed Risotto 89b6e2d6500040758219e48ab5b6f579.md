# Sunflower Seed Risotto

Cook time min: 20
Course: Veg
Ingredients: Soaked sunflower kernels 200 g. Onion 60 g. Veg jus 700 mL. Lemon 15 g. Dill oil 10 mL.
Method: Sweat onion. Add seeds. Add hot jus gradually 20 min. Finish lemon + dill oil.
Prep time min: 10
Status: Approved
Technique: Absorption
Yield g: 600
