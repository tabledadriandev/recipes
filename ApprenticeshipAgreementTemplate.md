## Terms & Conditions (Key Clauses)

1. **Educational Focus**: The primary purpose is education and skill development in precision culinary techniques (e.g., sous-vide, fermentation, sauce reduction).

2. **Confidentiality**: The Apprentice is bound by the terms of the Non-Disclosure Agreement.

3. **Duties**: Duties include mise en place, station maintenance, and assisting with service, always under supervision.

4. **Evaluation**: Performance will be reviewed monthly using the **\[Performance Evaluation Form]**.

5. **Termination**: The agreement may be terminated by either party with 7 days notice.
