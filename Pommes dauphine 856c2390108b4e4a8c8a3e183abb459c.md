# Pommes dauphine

Notes: Mash + pâte à choux, piped and fried to hollow puffs.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 2
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Fry 170–180
Time: 30–45 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Mix equal parts dry mash and choux paste; season.

2) Pipe quenelles; fry 170–180 °C until puffed and golden.

3) Drain; salt lightly.

### Notes

- Keep choux on the dry side for structure.
