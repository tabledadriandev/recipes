# Lemon gel (pectin NH)

Notes: Pectin NH‑set lemon gel; re‑meltable for piping.
Salt %: 0.05
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Boil 100; set 2–4 °C
Time: 2–4 h set
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Blend sugar with pectin NH; whisk into lemon juice + water.

2) Boil 1 min; pour to set.

3) Blend to smooth; adjust sweetness.

### Notes

- Reheat to loosen for dots.
