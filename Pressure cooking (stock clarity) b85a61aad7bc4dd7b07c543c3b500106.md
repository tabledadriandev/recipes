# Pressure cooking (stock clarity)

Applications: Vegetable and meat stocks
Confidence: 85
Corrections: Rinse bones, avoid over-roast, fine strain
Failure modes: Cloudy, bitter
Medium: Water
Notes: Quick chill to avoid off-notes.
Sensory cue: Deep flavor, clean finish
Source: EMP
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 118–120 (1 bar)
Time range: 30–60 min
Tools used: Pressure cooker
Type: Extract

### Method

1) Load rinsed bones/veg; cover with cold water. 2) Cook at 1 bar (118–120 °C) for 30–60 min. 3) Depressurize; strain fine; chill fast.

### Notes

- Sensory: deep flavor, clean finish; minimal bitterness.
- Test: roast level and time under pressure; acceptance = clear, no muddy notes.
- Uses: rapid stocks and fonds.
