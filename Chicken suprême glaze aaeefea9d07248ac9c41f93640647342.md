# Chicken suprême glaze

Notes: Poultry velouté reduced and finished with cream/butter to nappe for chicken.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Reduce 90–95
Time: 30–45 min
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Reduce poultry velouté gently to near nappe.

2) Add a splash of cream; simmer 2–3 min.

3) Mount with butter; strain; season.

### Notes

- Lemon drop at finish to lift.
