# Quality Targets (Nappé, Doneness, Skin, Acid)

## Nappé

- Clean line on spoon.

- Mirror gloss.

- No visible bubbles.

## Protein Doneness

- Gentle translucence for white fish.

- Rosy center for red meats (unless specified).

## Skin

- Dry and crisp.

- Audible crackle under knife.

## Acid Balance

- Finish until saliva response, then back off with fat.

- Seasoning: Taste post-acid; most sauces land at **0.7–0.9% salt**.
