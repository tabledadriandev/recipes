# Sauce Américaine (shellfish)

Notes: Classic lobster/crab prawn sauce; roast shells for depth.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Sauté 160–180; simmer 90–95
Time: 90–120 min
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Roast crushed shellfish shells with aromatics in oil at 180 °C to deep red.

2) Add tomato paste; sauté to caramelize. Deglaze with wine; reduce by half.

3) Cover with water; simmer 90–120 min. Strain fine; reduce to nappe. Mount with butter.

### Notes

- For bisque base, add rice in simmer and blend; for sauce, keep clear and mount.
