# Lacto-Blueberry Gastrique

## Ingredients

- 200 mL lacto-blueberry brine

- 200 mL perry vinegar

- 80 g sugar

## Process

1. Reduce all ingredients together until syrup coats a spoon.

2. Balance acidity with a drop of kombucha if needed.

## Parameters

- **Reduction**: Until syrup coats a spoon

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: deep violet gastrique; use with beet, plum, or celeriac.

## Creative Application

Drizzle the deep violet gastrique over roasted celeriac or a beet tartare for a sweet-sour, earthy counterpoint.
