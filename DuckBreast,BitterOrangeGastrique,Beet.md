# Duck Breast, Bitter Orange Gastrique, Beet

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Score skin; 1% salt 12 h uncovered.

- **Cook**: Render skin slowly; flip 1–2 min; **54–56 °C core**.

- **Rest**: 10 min.

## Sauce & Finish

- **Sauce**: Sugar caramel → deglaze orange + vinegar → add duck jus; reduce (Gastrique).

- **Finish**: Beet lacquer, pepper.

## Parameters

- **Brine Salt**: 1%

- **Core Temp**: 54–56 °C

- **Rest Time**: 10 min

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Crisp skin, rosy center.
