Lemon & Meringue Craquelin 


Craquelin Dough Preparation
	1.	Heat in a pot: milk, water, and butter. Add sugar and salt.
	2.	When it starts to boil, add the flour and whisk very well until you have a fine dough. Let it cook for 1 minute on low heat.
	3.	Transfer to a stand mixer, work the dough until it cools slightly. Add eggs one by one, mixing until the right consistency.
	4.	Pipe onto a baking tray using a sac à poche.
	5.	Place a disc of Craquelin on top.
	6.	Bake at 160°C (ventilated) for 35–40 minutes.

Lemon Curd

Ingredients
	•	4 lemons (zest + juice)
	•	90 g sugar
	•	100 g butter
	•	50 g water
	•	3 egg yolks
	•	25 g corn starch

Preparation
	1.	Grate the zest of all lemons, juice them, and place in a pot with the sugar.
	2.	Mix cornstarch with water and add to pot. Cook until thick.
	3.	Add egg yolks, cook 1 minute while whisking.
	4.	Remove from heat, add butter, and whisk until fully emulsified.
	5.	Cover with plastic foil and let cool completely before using.

⸻

Italian Meringue

Ingredients
	•	100 g egg whites
	•	250 g sugar
	•	40 g water

Preparation
	1.	Cook sugar and water in a pot. When the syrup reaches 110°C, begin beating the egg whites.
	2.	At 121°C, remove syrup from heat and pour gradually into the beating whites.
	3.	Continue beating until stiff and glossy. Ready when thick and holds shape. Transfer to a piping bag.

⸻

Assembly
	1.	Fill each Craquelin with the lemon curd.
	2.	Pipe the meringue on top.
	3.	Lightly brûlée the top with a blowtorch.
	4.	Serve and enjoy!
