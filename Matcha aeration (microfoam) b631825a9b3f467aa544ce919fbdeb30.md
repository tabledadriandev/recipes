# Matcha aeration (microfoam)

Applications: Sauces, desserts
Confidence: 70
Corrections: Lower temp, add lecithin 0.2–0.3%
Failure modes: Large bubbles, dull color
Medium: Air
Notes: Use soft water to avoid scum.
Sensory cue: Fine foam, vivid green
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 60–70
Time range: 1–3 min
Tools used: Hand blender, whisk
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Whisk matcha with 60–70 °C water; shear to microfoam. 2) Optionally add 0.2–0.3% lecithin for stability. 3) Use immediately.

### Notes

- Sensory: fine foam, vivid green; avoid large bubbles.
- Test: temp and lecithin; acceptance = fine foam persisting 2–3 min.
- Uses: sauces, dessert finishes.
