# Sauce Bordelaise

Notes: Red wine + shallot reduction finished into brown veal jus; marrow optional.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Reduce 90–95
Time: 45–75 min
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Reduce red wine with minced shallots, pepper, and thyme to a syrup.

2) Add brown veal jus; reduce to light nappe.

3) Strain; mount with butter. Optional: glazed marrow dice at service.

### Notes

- Balance acidity before service; avoid over‑reduction bitterness.
