# Citrus Blossom Spray

## Technique

Simmer zest in water for 3 min → chill → strain → load in atomizer.

## Parameters

- **Simmer Time**: 3 min

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Volatile aromatic spray for tableside finishing (1 spray).
