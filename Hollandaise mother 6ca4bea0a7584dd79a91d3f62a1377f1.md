# Hollandaise mother

Notes: Sabatier whisked or siphon. Lemon to taste. Danger above 70 °C (scramble).
Salt %: 0.8
Shelf Life (days): 0
Storage: Hold 57–60 °C
Temp °C: 62–68 (sauce temp)
Time: 10–15 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1. Whisk yolks with reduction over a bain‑marie at low heat.
2. When ribbons form, slowly whisk in warm clarified butter.
3. Adjust with lemon, salt. Hold at 57–60 °C.

### Notes

- Avoid >70 °C to prevent scrambling.
- Add warm water to loosen if too thick.
