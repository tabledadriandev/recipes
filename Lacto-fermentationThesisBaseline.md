# Lacto-Fermentation: Thesis & Baseline

**Thesis**: Vegetables and fruits are transformed by Lactic Acid Bacteria (LAB) using 2% salt and the exclusion of oxygen.

**Baseline Playbook**:

1. Trim, pack, and add **2% salt** by total produce weight.

2. Submerge fully and exclude oxygen.

3. Ferment at room temp, burping or using an airlock.

4. Chill when desired acidity is reached (the "kill switch").

**Safety Rail**: Use 2% salt by weight, submerge, and vent CO₂.
