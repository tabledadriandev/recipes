# Beurre monté poaching (protein)

Applications: Lobster, white fish, asparagus
Confidence: 85
Corrections: Lower heat, add water, smaller cubes
Failure modes: Split, overcook
Medium: Fat
Notes: Gentle agitation only.
Sensory cue: Translucent edges, moist core
Source: EMP
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 58–62
Time range: 8–20 min
Tools used: Saucepan, thermometer
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Prepare beurre monté. 2) Submerge or baste protein at 58–62 °C until target core temp. 3) Rest briefly; glaze with pan film.

### Notes

- Ratio: water in monté 5–10%. Sensory: glossy coat, no split.
- Test: hold 58 vs 60 vs 62 °C; acceptance = stable film and desired doneness.
- Uses: lobster tails, turbot, asparagus tips.
