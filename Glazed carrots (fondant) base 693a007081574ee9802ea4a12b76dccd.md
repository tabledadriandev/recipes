# Glazed carrots (fondant) base

Notes: Butter, water, pinch of sugar; finish with monté.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 2
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 85–95
Time: 15–25 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1. Arrange carrots, add water to halfway, butter, salt, pinch sugar.
2. Simmer covered until tender; uncover and reduce to glaze.
3. Finish with beurre monté to shine.

### Notes

- Keep heat moderate to avoid scorching.
