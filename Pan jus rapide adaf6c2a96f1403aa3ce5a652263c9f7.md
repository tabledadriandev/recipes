# Pan jus rapide

Notes: For service à la minute.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Deglaze boil; finish 85–90
Time: 10–20 min
Type: Jus

### Method

1. Deglaze roasting pan with wine or stock; scrape fond.
2. Add a ladle of stock; reduce to light coat.
3. Mount with cold butter; season to taste; strain.

### Notes

- Avoid over-reduction to prevent bitterness.
