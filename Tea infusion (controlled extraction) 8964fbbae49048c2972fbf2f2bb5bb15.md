# Tea infusion (controlled extraction)

Applications: Broths, glazes, desserts
Confidence: 80
Corrections: Lower temp, shorter time, larger leaf
Failure modes: Bitterness, dull aroma
Medium: Water
Notes: Bloom aromatics separately for clarity.
Sensory cue: Clear liquor, no astringent bite
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 60–95 by tea
Time range: 2–8 min
Tools used: Kettle, timer, filter
Type: Infusion

### Method

1) Heat water to target (60–95 °C by tea). 2) Steep 2–8 min; separate leaves quickly. 3) Use liquor directly or as infusion base.

### Notes

- Sensory: clear liquor, no astringent bite. Lower temp and shorter time for delicate teas.
- Test: time × temp grid; acceptance = clean aroma, no bitterness.
- Uses: broths, glazes, desserts.
