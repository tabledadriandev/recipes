# Citrus oil expression (zest infusion)

Applications: Finishing oils, emulsions
Confidence: 80
Corrections: Avoid pith, lower temp
Failure modes: Waxiness, bitterness
Medium: Fat
Notes: Short infusion preserves freshness.
Sensory cue: Bright top notes, no pith bitterness
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 20–50
Time range: 15–60 min
Tools used: Zester, jar
Type: Infuse

**Principle**

Expressed citrus oils infuse into fat at low temperature without pith bitterness.

- Parameters
    - Temp: 20–50 °C
    - Time: 15–60 min
    - Zest only (no pith)
- Goal
    - Aroma: bright top notes
    - Flavor: no waxy bitterness

<aside>
⚠️

Sensory benchmark: clean citrus nose; finish free of pith

</aside>

- Test
    - Variable: temp and time
    - Observe: intensity, bitterness threshold
    - Acceptance: target intensity without bitterness at 30 min
- Uses
    - Finishing oils, emulsions
