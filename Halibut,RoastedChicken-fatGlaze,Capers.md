# Halibut, Roasted Chicken-Fat Glaze, Capers

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Brine **1.5% salt 30 min**; dry.

- **Cook**: Pan roast.

## Glaze & Finish

- **Glaze**: Clarified chicken fat + capers + lemon.

## Parameters

- **Brine Salt/Time**: 1.5% / 30 min

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Pearly, just-set lamellae.
