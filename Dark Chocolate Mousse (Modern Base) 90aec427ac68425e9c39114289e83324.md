# Dark Chocolate Mousse (Modern Base)

Cook time min: 10
Course: Dessert
Ingredients: Dark chocolate 300 g. Hot cream 150 mL. Whipped cream 250 mL. Yolks 40 g. Sugar 40 g.
Method: Sabayon 82 °C. Add melted chocolate + hot cream. Cool to 35 °C. Fold cream. Set 3 h.
Prep time min: 20
Status: Approved
Technique: Sabayon + fold
Yield g: 900
