# Bouillabaisse Reduction, Saffron-Rouille

## Technique

Roast fish bones + aromatics + tomato → add shell trimmings → simmer 45 min → strain → reduce **50–70%**.

## Finish

- Serve as sauce with roasted white fish.

- Saffron rouille quenelle.

## Parameters

- **Simmer Time**: 45 min

- **Reduction**: 50–70%

## Plating Cues

- **Viscosity**: Reduced to a sauce consistency.
