# Hollandaise (hot emulsion)

Applications: Eggs, fish, veg
Confidence: 85
Corrections: Cool to 55–60 °C, add reduction or water to adjust
Failure modes: Scramble, thinning
Medium: Fat
Notes: Hold 57–60 °C. Avoid >70 °C.
Sensory cue: Ribbons hold, light lemon aroma
Source: EMP
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 62–68 sauce temp
Time range: 10–15 min
Tools used: Bain‑marie, whisk
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Build reduction. 2) Whisk yolks over bain‑marie to ribbon at 62–68 °C. 3) Stream in warm clarified butter while whisking. 4) Adjust with lemon and water to texture. Hold 57–60 °C.

### Notes

- Sensory: ribbons hold 2–3 s, no graininess.
- Test: hold at 57, 60, 63 °C and check 20‑min stability.
- Uses: asparagus, poached salmon, veal blanquette finish.
