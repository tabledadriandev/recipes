# Chicken Ballotine, Black Truffle Jus

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Debone; fill with mushroom duxelles; wrap in skin; tie.

- **Cook**: Sous-vide **63 °C for 2 h** → chill → sear skin crisp in butter.

## Sauce & Finish

- **Sauce**: Truffle jus from roasted carcasses; mount (Jus Court technique).

## Parameters

- **SV Temp/Time**: 63 °C / 2 h

- **HACCP**: Hold at 63 °C for 30 min minimum (Control Panel standard).

## Plating Cues

- **Finish**: Cylinder slices, juices clear.
