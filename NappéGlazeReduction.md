# Nappé Glaze Reduction

## Technique

Reduce vegetable jus + 4–6% glucose + a few drops vinegar to a syrup point of 103–105 °C.

## Parameters

- **Glucose %**: 4–6%

- **Target Temp**: 103–105 °C

- **Viscosity**: Nappé (coats spoon, clean line when finger drawn)
