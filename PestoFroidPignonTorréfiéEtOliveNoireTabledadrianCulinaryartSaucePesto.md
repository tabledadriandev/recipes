# Pesto Froid: Pignon Torréfié et Olive Noire #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Sauce #Pesto

**Preface:** Two distinct, cold-set pestos, designed for precise application, offering concentrated notes of nut and Mediterranean salinity.

**Ingredients**

- **Toasted Pine Nut Pesto (Fluid):**

  - 150 g toasted pine nuts

  - 200 g heavy cream

  - 100 g UHT milk

  - 25 g toasted bread (crust removed)

  - 10 g olive oil

- **Black Olive Pesto (Concentrated):**

  - 150 g Taggiasca olives in oil (drained)

  - 30 g toasted pine nuts

  - 10 g garlic

  - 1 sprig fresh rosemary

**Method**

1. **Pine Nut Pesto:** Toast the pine nuts in butter until golden. Cool completely.

2. Blend the toasted pine nuts, toasted bread, milk, cream, and olive oil until a perfectly smooth, fluid paste is achieved. Strain if necessary for pipette use.

3. **Black Olive Pesto:** Combine the Taggiasca olives, toasted pine nuts, garlic, and rosemary. Blend until a concentrated, uniform paste is achieved.

4. Store both pestos chilled in airtight containers.

**Technical Notes:** The inclusion of milk and cream in the pine nut pesto ensures a fluid viscosity suitable for precise plating techniques.\
**Chef’s Intention:** These concentrated condiments provide disciplined, regional flavour accents to a finished plate.\
