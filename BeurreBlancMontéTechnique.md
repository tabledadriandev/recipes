# Beurre Blanc / Monté Technique

## Technique

Reduce wine + vinegar + shallot to a glaze → whisk in cold butter off heat (Beurre Blanc) OR use plant oil monté variant.

## Parameters

- **Reduction**: Reduce wine/vinegar to a glaze.

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Rich, emulsified sauce.
