# Tomato concassé

Notes: Seeded, skinned dice.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 2
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Blanch 95–100; chill 0–3
Time: 10–20 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1. Score tomatoes; blanch 10–20 s; shock in ice.
2. Peel, seed, dice; season lightly.
3. Drain excess juice before service.

### Notes

- Choose ripe but firm tomatoes.
