# Spring Pea Custard, Mint Gelée, Pickled Pea Shoots

## Components

- Pea purée kappa-iota custard

- Mint fluid gel

- Light pickled shoots

## Method

- Set custard in shallow dish.

- Dot gel.

- Crown with shoots.

## Parameters

- **Custard Gelling Agents**: Kappa and Iota Carrageenan

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Trembling set (custard).

- **Color**: Electric green.

- **Service**: Cold service.

## Creative Pairing

A cold, vibrant spring dish contrasting the silken, savory pea custard with the trembling, fresh mint gelée and the sharp crunch of pickled pea shoots.
