# Hot infusion oil (spice)

Applications: Chili oil, paprika oil
Confidence: 85
Corrections: Lower temp, shorten contact, strain promptly
Failure modes: Scorched spices
Medium: Fat
Notes: Test with paper spot for color.
Sensory cue: Bloomed spice aroma, vivid hue
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 120–150
Time range: 5–20 min
Tools used: Saucepan, thermometer
Type: Infusion

### Method

1) Heat neutral oil with spices at 120–150 °C until aroma blooms. 2) Remove from heat; steep 5–20 min; strain through fine filter.

### Notes

- Sensory: vivid hue, bloomed spice aroma; no scorched notes.
- Test: temp 120 vs 135 vs 150 °C; acceptance = bright color, no bitterness.
- Uses: chili oil, paprika oil.
