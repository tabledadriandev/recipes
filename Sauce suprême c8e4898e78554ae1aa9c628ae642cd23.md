# Sauce suprême

Notes: Poultry velouté finished with cream and butter; classic for chicken.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 85–92; finish 55–60
Time: 25–35 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Reduce poultry velouté to nappant.

2) Stir in warm cream; simmer gently 2–3 min.

3) Off heat, mount with butter; strain; adjust salt and lemon.

### Notes

- Keep cream gentle to avoid splitting; holds short at 57–60 °C.
