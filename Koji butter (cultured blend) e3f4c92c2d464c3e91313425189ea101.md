# Koji butter (cultured blend)

Applications: Finishes, crumbs, sauces
Confidence: 65
Corrections: Shorten culture, adjust salt
Failure modes: Over-acid, separation
Medium: Fat
Notes: Blend butter with koji paste and culture.
Sensory cue: Savory, slight tang, spreadable
Source: Noma
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 20–30 culture; 4 hold
Time range: 12–48 h
Tools used: Blender, incubator
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Blend softened butter with koji paste and salt. 2) Culture at 20–30 °C 12–48 h to develop savory notes. 3) Chill; use as finish or in crumbs.

### Notes

- Sensory: savory, slight tang; spreadable. Avoid over‑acid.
- Test: culture time; acceptance = pleasant tang, no separation.
- Uses: finishes, crumbs, sauces.
