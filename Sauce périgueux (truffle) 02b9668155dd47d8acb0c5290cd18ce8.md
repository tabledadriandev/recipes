# Sauce périgueux (truffle)

Notes: Demi‑glace with madeira and black truffle; finish with truffle brunoise.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Reduce 90–95
Time: 20–30 min
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Reduce madeira by half.

2) Add demi‑glace; simmer to nappant.

3) Stir in fine truffle brunoise and a knob of butter just before service.

### Notes

- Do not boil after truffle is added to preserve aroma.
