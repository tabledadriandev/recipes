# Sweet potato miso (long ferment)

Applications: Sauces, glazes, desserts
Confidence: 60
Corrections: Salt cover, scrape, longer age
Failure modes: Surface mold, harshness
Medium: Water
Notes: Salt 5–8% of total.
Sensory cue: Umami, sweet aroma, paste texture
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 28–32
Time range: 2–6 mo
Tools used: Crocks, weights
Type: Fermentation

### Method

1) Cook sweet potato; mash with koji paste and salt 5–8%. 2) Pack into crock; weight and cover. 3) Ferment 28–32 °C for 2–6 months; stir monthly.

### Notes

- Sensory: umami, sweet aroma, smooth paste. Surface mold: scrape and salt cover.
- Test: salt % and time; acceptance = clean, savory paste without harshness.
- Uses: sauces, glazes, desserts.
