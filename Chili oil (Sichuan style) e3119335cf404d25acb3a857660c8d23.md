# Chili oil (Sichuan style)

Notes: Toast aromatics; pour hot oil over chili flakes.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 30
Storage: 0–20 °C
Temp °C: 150–180 pour-over
Time: 20–30 min
Type: Oil

### Method

1. Heat neutral oil with aromatics to 150–180 °C; remove aromatics.
2. Pour oil over chili flakes in heatproof bowl; stir.
3. Cool; bottle; rest 24 h for bloom.

### Notes

- Control temp to avoid burnt notes.
