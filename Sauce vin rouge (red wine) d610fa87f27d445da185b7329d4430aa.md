# Sauce vin rouge (red wine)

Notes: Shallot-wine reduction bound with brown veal jus; classic for beef and game.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Reduce 90–95
Time: 45–75 min
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Reduce red wine with minced shallots, cracked pepper, thyme, bay to a light syrup.

2) Add brown veal jus; simmer to light nappe, skimming.

3) Strain fine; mount with butter just before service.

### Notes

- Balance acidity with a touch of demi or a knob of butter; avoid bitterness from over‑reduction.
