# Control Panel: Brines, Cures, Sous-Vide, Resting

## Equilibrium Brines (Meat/Poultry)

- **Salt %**: 0.8–1.5%

- **Sugar %**: 0.3–0.8%

- **Time/Temp**: 12–24 h at 2–4 °C.

## Quick Cures (Fish/Crudo)

- **Salt %**: 1–2%

- **Time**: 10–20 min

- **Finish**: Rinse or wipe.

## Dry-Age (Beef/Lamb)

- **Temp/Humidity**: 0–2 °C, 75–85% RH

- **Airflow**: Steady

- **Time**: 21–45 d typical.

## Sous-Vide Core Targets

- **Rare Beef**: 50–52 °C

- **Medium Beef**: 55–57 °C

- **Chicken**: 62–64 °C (hold ≥30 min)

- **Pork**: 58–60 °C

- **Salmon Mi-Cuit**: 42–45 °C

## Pan Roast Sear

- **Pan Temp**: 200–210 °C surface (steel pan, neutral oil)

- **Sear Time**: 60–90 s per side

- **Finish Temp**: 160–180 °C oven.

## Resting Times

- **Small Cuts**: 3–5 min

- **Roasts**: 10–20 min

- **Birds**: 20–30 min (loosely tented).

## Fish Skin Crisp

- **Prep**: Score, dry 12–24 h uncovered, salt to order.

- **Cook**: 80–90% of cook skin-side, baste late.
