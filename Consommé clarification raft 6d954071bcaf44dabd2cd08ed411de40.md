# Consommé clarification raft

Notes: Lean ground meat + mirepoix brunoise + egg whites 1–2/ L. Do not boil. Cut vent and baste.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 0
Storage: Use fresh
Temp °C: 82–88 (no boil)
Time: 45–90 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1. Combine lean ground meat, mirepoix brunoise, egg whites (1–2 per L), cold stock.
2. Heat gently to 82–88 °C without boiling; form raft.
3. Cut a vent; baste liquid over raft until clear.
4. Ladle clarified consommé through vent; strain fine.

### Notes

- Do not boil or the consommé will cloud.
- Salt only after clarification.
