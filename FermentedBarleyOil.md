# Fermented Barley Oil

## Ingredients

- 500 mL neutral oil

- 100 g roasted, koji-fermented barley

## Process

1. Infuse at 60 °C for 3 h.

2. Strain through coffee filter; store cold.

## Parameters

- **Temperature**: 60 °C (infusion)

- **Time**: 3 h (infusion)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: nutty, malted oil with subtle sweetness; ideal for emulsions or roasted roots.

## Creative Application

Use the malted oil as the fat base for a shio-koji emulsion to dress roasted root vegetables, enhancing the nutty flavor profile.
