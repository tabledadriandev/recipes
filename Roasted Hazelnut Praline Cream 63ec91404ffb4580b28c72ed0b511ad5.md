# Roasted Hazelnut Praline Cream

Cook time min: 25
Course: Dessert
Ingredients: Hazelnuts 200 g. Sugar 100 g. Water 20 mL. Milk 300 mL. Cream 150 mL. Yolks 60 g. Starch 10 g.
Method: Caramel nuts to praline paste. Cook custard 82–84 °C. Add praline.
Prep time min: 20
Status: Approved
Technique: Caramel + custard
Yield g: 700
