# Apple “Tatin” with Buckwheat Feuille de Brick

## Components

- Compressed apple fans

- Buckwheat brick (feuille de brick)

- Apple caramel

- Rosemary vapor

## Method

- Layer, bake crisp.

- Nappé caramel.

- Rosemary atomizer at table.

## Parameters

- **Apple Prep**: Compressed

- **Service**: Rosemary atomizer at table

## Plating Cues

- **Form**: Classic form, plant technique.

## Creative Pairing

A plant-based dessert that uses compressed apple for texture and a buckwheat tuile for crispness, finished tableside with a burst of rosemary vapor for aromatic lift.
