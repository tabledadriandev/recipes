# Beurre blanc base

Notes: Reduce vinegar/shallot until syrupy, mount cold butter off heat. No boil after mounting.
Salt %: 0.8
Shelf Life (days): 2
Storage: Hold warm or chill rapid
Temp °C: 55–60 hold
Time: 10–20 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1. Reduce white wine vinegar and minced shallots to a light syrup.
2. Off heat, whisk in cold butter cubes gradually to emulsify.
3. Hold at 55–60 °C, whisking gently. Do not boil after mounting.

### Notes

- Finish with a splash of cream if extra stability is needed.
