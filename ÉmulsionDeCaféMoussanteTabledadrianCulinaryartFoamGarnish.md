# Émulsion de Café Moussante #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Foam #Garnish

**Preface:** A light, aerated emulsion, designed as a foamy garnish to deliver a concentrated coffee aroma and a delicate texture.• 1 tsp soy lecithin (optional)

**Ingredients**

- **Base:**

  - 1 long espresso (concentrated)

  - 50 g heavy cream (35% fat)

  - 1 tsp soy lecithin (optional, for stabilization)

  - Fine salt and white pepper (to season)

**Method**

1. Combine the concentrated coffee, heavy cream, lecithin (if used), salt, and pepper in a small saucepan.

2. Heat the mixture gently to precisely 80°C, stirring to ensure the lecithin is dissolved.

3. Use an immersion blender, held half-submerged, to aerate and emulsify the mixture, creating a stable foam.

4. Serve immediately as a foamy topping or garnish.

**Technical Notes:** The precise temperature of 80°C is necessary to activate the lecithin and stabilize the cream proteins for optimal foam structure.\
**Chef’s Intention:** A disciplined, aromatic foam that provides a light, textural finish to a dessert or savoury course.\
