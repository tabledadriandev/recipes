# Sabayon (warm)

Notes: Foamed yolk emulsion with wine/sugar; savory or sweet.
Salt %: 0.5
Shelf Life (days): 0
Storage: Hold 57–60 °C
Temp °C: 62–68 (sauce temp)
Time: 8–12 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Whisk yolks with wine (or stock) and sugar/salt over bain‑marie to ribbons at 62–68 °C.

2) Adjust with reduction or stock; finish with a touch of butter or cream if desired.

3) Serve immediately; hold 57–60 °C briefly.

### Notes

- Avoid scrambling; constant whisking and controlled heat are key.
