# Lacto-Fermented Plums

## Ingredients

- 1 kg ripe plums, washed and halved

- 20 g fine sea salt

## Process

1. Mix salt into plums until evenly coated.

2. Pack tightly into a jar; submerge fully in juice.

3. Seal with airlock or burp daily for 7 days.

4. Refrigerate when desired flavor is reached.

## Parameters

- **Salt %**: 2%

- **Temperature**: \~20–25 °C (room temperature)

- **Time**: 7 days (fermentation)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: tart-salty fruit for sauces or umami glazes.

- **Stop Cue**: When sharp and floral.

## Creative Application

Use the tart-salty plum liquid as a base for a savory fruit vinaigrette to dress grilled duck breast.
