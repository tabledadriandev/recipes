# Beurre de Crevette et Son Alternative Végétale #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Butter #Umami

**Preface:** A compound butter designed to deliver a concentrated, marine umami, with a precise plant-based alternative for dietary flexibility.

**Ingredients**

- **Prawn Powder (Original):**

  - 300 g fresh shrimp (shells and heads)

  - Parsley stalks, lemon/orange zest, bay leaf, spices (for drying)

- **Prawn Butter (Original):**

  - 100 g unsalted butter (softened)

  - 20 g prawn powder

  - 2 g verjus

- **Vegan Alternative:**

  - Vegan butter (softened)

  - Smoked paprika, nori powder, dried tomato powder (for umami)

**Method**

1. **Prawn Powder:** Dry the shrimp components with aromatics until completely brittle. Mill into a fine powder.

2. **Prawn Butter:** Combine the softened butter, prawn powder, and verjus. Mix until homogenous. Form into a disciplined log and chill.

3. **Vegan Alternative:** Combine the softened vegan butter with the smoked paprika, nori powder, and dried tomato powder. Mix until the umami profile is balanced. Chill.

**Technical Notes:** The drying process for the prawn powder must be complete to ensure a stable, concentrated flavour without moisture.\
**Chef’s Intention:** A concentrated, umami-rich component that provides a disciplined depth of flavour to seafood or vegetable preparations.\
