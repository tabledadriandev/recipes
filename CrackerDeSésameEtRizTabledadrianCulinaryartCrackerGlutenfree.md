# Cracker de Sésame et Riz #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Cracker #GlutenFree

**Preface:** A crisp, savoury cracker, relying on rice flour and tahini for a robust, gluten-free structure and deep sesame flavour.• Optional: sesame seeds, za’atar, cumin, rosemary, or nigella seeds for topping

**Ingredients**

- **Base (Yields 25):**

  - 100 g rice flour

  - 30 g runny tahini

  - 1 tbsp Ligurian olive oil

  - ½ tsp fine salt

  - ¼ tsp baking powder (optional)

  - 3–5 tbsp water (tepid)

- **Garnish (Optional):** Sesame seeds, za’atar, cumin, rosemary, or nigella seeds

**Method**

1. Combine the rice flour, salt, and baking powder.

2. Incorporate the tahini and olive oil until the mixture resembles coarse sand.

3. Add the water gradually until a firm, non-sticky dough is formed.

4. Rest the dough for 15 minutes. Roll thinly between two sheets of parchment paper.

5. Cut into desired shapes and dock the surface. Brush with water and apply the chosen garnish.

6. Bake at 180°C until crisp and golden.

**Technical Notes:** The dough must be rolled to a uniform thinness to ensure even crispness across the batch.\
**Chef’s Intention:** A disciplined, textural component that carries the aromatic profile of the Mediterranean.\
