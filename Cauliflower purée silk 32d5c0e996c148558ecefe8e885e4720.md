# Cauliflower purée silk

Notes: Cook florets in milk/water; blend hot with butter; strain fine.
Salt %: 1.2
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 90–95; blend 80
Time: 35–50 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Simmer cauliflower with a pinch of salt until very tender.

2) Drain (reserve some liquor); blend hot with butter; adjust with liquor.

3) Pass through fine sieve; season.

### Notes

- Add a few drops of lemon to lift; avoid grain by fully cooking.
