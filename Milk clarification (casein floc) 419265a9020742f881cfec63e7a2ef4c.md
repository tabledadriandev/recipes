Confidence: 75
Corrections: Add acid, adjust temp, gentle stir
Failure modes: No floc, grainy texture

Sensory cue: Floc formation, clear whey
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 60–85
Time range: 10–30 min
Tools used: Saucepan, filter
