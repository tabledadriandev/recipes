# Brown veal jus

Notes: Roast bones and mirepoix deeply. Deglaze, reduce to light coat. Salt after reduction.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Roast 200 °C; simmer 90 °C
Time: 6–8 h total
Type: Jus

### Method

1. Roast veal bones and mirepoix at 200 °C until deep brown.
2. Deglaze pans; combine with cold water.
3. Simmer 90 °C, skimming; add tomato paste lightly.
4. Reduce to light nappe; strain fine; chill.

### Notes

- Season only after final reduction.
- Clarify if muddy flavor persists.
