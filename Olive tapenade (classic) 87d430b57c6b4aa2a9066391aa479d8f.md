# Olive tapenade (classic)

Notes: Black olives, capers, anchovy, garlic, lemon; paste for canapés and sauces.
Salt %: 1.5
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Cold prep
Time: 15–20 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Blend pitted black olives with capers, anchovy, garlic, lemon juice/zest.

2) Stream in olive oil to a spreadable paste.

3) Season to taste; fold in chopped parsley.

### Notes

- Rinse capers/anchovies to control salt; adjust with acid.
