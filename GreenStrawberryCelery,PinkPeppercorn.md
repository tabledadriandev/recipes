# Green Strawberry & Celery, Pink Peppercorn

## Components

- Lacto green strawberries

- Celery batons

- Pink pepper syrup

- Celery leaf oil

## Method

- Assemble chilled.

- Finish syrup threads.

## Parameters

- **Strawberry Prep**: Lacto-fermented (Lacto Baseline technique)

- **Service**: Chilled

## Plating Cues

- **Flavor**: Tart, spicy, herbaceous.

## Creative Pairing

A bright, acidic, and herbaceous palate cleanser that contrasts the tartness of lacto-fermented green strawberries with the volatile spice of pink peppercorn syrup.
