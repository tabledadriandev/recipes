# Poached eggs (service)

Notes: Crack into vortex; set whites, runny yolk; shock and reheat for service.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 1
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 82–90
Time: 3–4 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Simmer water with pinch salt and splash vinegar.

2) Create vortex; slide egg in; cook 3–4 min.

3) Shock in ice; trim; reheat 60–65 °C to serve.

### Notes

- Use very fresh eggs for tight whites.
