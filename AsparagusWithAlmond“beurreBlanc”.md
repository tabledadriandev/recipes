# Asparagus with Almond “Beurre Blanc”

## Components

- Charred white asparagus

- Almond milk reduction

- Lemon oil

- Toasted almond crumbs

## Method

- Reduce almond milk with shallot to ⅓ volume.

- Mount oil with shear (Plant Beurre Monté technique).

- Glaze spears.

## Parameters

- **Reduction**: ⅓ volume

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Nappé body without heaviness.

- **Finish**: Bright acidity (from lemon oil).

## Creative Pairing

A dairy-free interpretation of a classic beurre blanc, using the richness of almond milk and oil to coat charred asparagus, finished with a sharp, bright lemon note.
