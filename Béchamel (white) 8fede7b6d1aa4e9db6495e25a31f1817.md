# Béchamel (white)

Notes: White roux with milk; base for Mornay and gratins.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 85–92
Time: 25–35 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Cook white roux 3–5 min without color.

2) Whisk in hot milk gradually; simmer 20–30 min.

3) Season with salt, white pepper, nutmeg.

### Notes

- Strain for extra smoothness.
