# Grilled Mackerel with Mustard Pickle Glaze

Cook time min: 6
Course: Fish
Ingredients: Mackerel 4×120 g. Pickled mustard seeds 30 g. Honey 15 g. Cider vinegar 10 mL.
Method: Brush paste. Grill skin-down 220 °C 5 min. Finish 1 min flesh. Brush again.
Prep time min: 10
Status: Approved
Technique: Grill + glaze
Yield g: 500
