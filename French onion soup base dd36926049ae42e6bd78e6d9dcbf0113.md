# French onion soup base

Notes: Deeply caramelized onions deglazed and simmered with brown stock; gratin ready.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 4
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Caramelize 150–170; simmer 90–95
Time: 2–3 h
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Slowly caramelize sliced onions in butter and a little oil until deep mahogany (60–90 min).

2) Deglaze with dry white wine; reduce by half.

3) Add brown beef/veal stock; simmer 45–60 min. Season, strain if desired.

### Notes

- A splash of sherry or cognac at finish; keep salt modest for gratin cheese.
