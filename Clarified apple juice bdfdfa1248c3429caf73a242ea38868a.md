# Clarified apple juice

Applications: Consommé, jelly base, sorbet base
Confidence: 85
Corrections: Increase enzyme contact, fine filtration, antioxidant
Failure modes: Haze persists, oxidation
Medium: Water
Notes: Antioxidant 0.1% ascorbic + 0.05% citric.
Related Preparations: Clarified apple stock (https://www.notion.so/Clarified-apple-stock-051ff0ddd36142b1942b901dc0ed6a27?pvs=21)
Sensory cue: Brilliant clarity, fresh aroma
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 2–4 or 40–50 (enzyme)
Time range: 2–24 h
Tools used: Pectinase, filter, cold room
Type: Clarify

### Method

1) Treat apple juice with 0.1–0.3% pectinase at 40–50 °C for 1–2 h. 2) Chill 2–4 °C and settle. 3) Filter fine until brilliant clarity.

### Notes

- Sensory: water‑clear, fresh aroma. Antioxidant 0.1% ascorbic + 0.05% citric to prevent browning.
- Uses: consommé base, jelly base, sorbet base.
