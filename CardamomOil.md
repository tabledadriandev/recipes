# Cardamom Oil

## Ingredients

- 100 g cardamom pods

- 300 g neutral oil

## Process

1. Low simmer for 30 min.

2. Lightly blend.

3. Rest 1 h.

4. Fine-strain.

## Parameters

- **Time**: 30 min (simmer), 1 h (rest)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Aromatic oil for seasoning and plating rituals.
