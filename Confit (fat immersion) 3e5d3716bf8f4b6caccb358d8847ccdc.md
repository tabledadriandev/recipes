# Confit (fat immersion)

Applications: Duck legs, garlic, fish
Confidence: 85
Corrections: Lower temp, ensure full coverage
Failure modes: Fry, dryness
Medium: Fat
Notes: Salt cure before cook for meats.
Sensory cue: Tender, moist, no browning
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 80–95
Time range: 2–6 h
Tools used: Pot, thermometer
Type: Confit

**Principle**

Submersion cook in fat below frying temperature for uniform tenderness.

- Parameters
    - Temp: 80–95 °C
    - Time: 2–6 h
    - Pre-cure: salt 1–2% for meats
- Goal
    - Texture: fork-tender, juicy
    - Visual: no browning during cook

<aside>
⚠️

Sensory benchmark: collagen breakdown without dryness

</aside>

- Test
    - Variable: temp 82 vs 88 vs 92 °C
    - Observe: tenderness score, moisture loss
    - Acceptance: tender with <10% loss
- Uses
    - Veg: garlic confit
    - Meat: duck legs
    - Fish: olive oil confit salmon
    - Dessert: citrus peel confit
