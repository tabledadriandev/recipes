# Biscuit Éponge à la Pistache Verte #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Dessert #Baking

**Preface:** A vibrant, intensely flavoured sponge, utilizing high-quality pistachio powder to achieve a striking green hue and concentrated nut essence.• 1.5 g baking powder

**Ingredients**

- **Base:**

  - 50 g extra green pistachios (finely milled)

  - 2 whole eggs

  - 25 g powdered sugar

  - 5 g all-purpose flour

  - 1.5 g baking powder

**Method**

1. Mill the pistachios into a fine powder to maximize colour and flavour extraction.

2. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and powdered sugar until the mixture is pale, voluminous, and forms a ribbon.

3. Gently fold in the pistachio powder, flour, and baking powder until the mixture is just combined, taking care not to deflate the aeration.

4. Spread the mixture onto a prepared baking tray or into a desired mold.

5. Bake at 170°C for 8–10 minutes until the sponge is set and lightly springy to the touch.

**Technical Notes:** The quality of the pistachio is paramount; the "extra green" variety ensures the desired visual depth.

**Chef’s Intention:** A disciplined, aromatic component that provides a textural contrast within a plated dessert.
