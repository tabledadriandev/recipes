# Cryo-shattering herbs

Applications: Powders, dusts, crusts
Confidence: 70
Corrections: Work fast, dust with maltodextrin
Failure modes: Clumping, oxidation
Medium: Air
Notes: Store airtight with desiccant.
Sensory cue: Fine fragrant powder
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): -18 to -40
Time range: 1–2 h
Tools used: Freezer, mortar
Type: Freeze

### Method

1) Freeze herbs at −18 to −40 °C for 1–2 h. 2) Shatter in mortar or processor quickly. 3) Optionally dust with maltodextrin; store airtight with desiccant.

### Notes

- Sensory: fine, fragrant powder; no clumping.
- Test: freeze time and storage; acceptance = free‑flowing after 24 h.
- Uses: powders, dusts, crusts.
