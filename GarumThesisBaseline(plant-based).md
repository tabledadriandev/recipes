# Garum: Thesis & Baseline (Plant-Based)

**Thesis**: Proteolysis in brine produces clear umami liquids; heat and salt ensure safety. Noma uses an accelerated method.

**Baseline Playbook (Plant Garum)**:

1. Base: toasted yeast, mushrooms, or nuts + koji + **\~12% salt** + water to slurry.

2. Ferment at **\~60 °C** covered until fragrant (accelerated method); or room temp for 8–9 months.

3. Strain; use the clear liquid.

**Safety Rail**: Use **\~12% salt**; use the **\~60 °C** accelerating method or room temp for 8–9 months; keep covered.
