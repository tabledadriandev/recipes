# Beurre monté

Applications: Glazing veg, poaching lobster, finishing sauces
Confidence: 90
Corrections: Lower heat, add fat, gentle agitation
Failure modes: Overheating, dilution, aggressive whisking
Medium: Fat
Notes: Use acidic splash to stabilize if needed.
Related Preparations: Beurre monté base (https://www.notion.so/Beurre-mont-base-023072990e4d46a088f57532926e0438?pvs=21)
Sensory cue: Glossy, nappe, no split
Source: EMP
Standardized: Yes
Temperature range (°C): 58–65
Time range: 5–15 min
Tools used: Saucepan, whisk, thermometer
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Simmer a small splash of water; off heat, whisk in cold butter cubes gradually to form satin emulsion. 2) Hold 58–65 °C; whisk gently.

### Notes

- Sensory: glossy, nappe, no split; acceptance = no visible oiling for 10 min at 60 °C.
- Test: hold temp 58/60/62 °C.
- Uses: glaze veg, poach lobster, finish sauces.
