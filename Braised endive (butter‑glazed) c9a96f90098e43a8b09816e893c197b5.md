# Braised endive (butter‑glazed)

Notes: Slow braise in butter, stock, and a touch of sugar; finish glazed.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 2
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 85–95
Time: 30–45 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Halve endives; lightly caramelize cut side in butter.

2) Add a splash of stock, pinch sugar, and salt; cover and braise until tender.

3) Uncover and reduce to glaze; season.

### Notes

- A few drops of lemon tame bitterness.
