# Garlic confit

Notes: Fully submerged in oil.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 14
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: 85–95
Time: 45–90 min
Type: Oil

### Method

1. Peel cloves; cover with neutral oil; add a pinch of salt.
2. Cook 85–95 °C until tender but intact.
3. Cool submerged; store refrigerated.

### Notes

- Keep cloves fully covered to prevent oxidation.
