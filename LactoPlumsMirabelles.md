# Lacto Plums / Mirabelles

## Ingredients

- 2 kg plums (halved)

- 40 g salt

## Process

1. Mix plums and salt.

2. Ferment 5 d at 28 °C.

3. Freeze juice.

4. Dry skins to powder when defrosted.

## Parameters

- **Salt %**: 2%

- **Temperature**: 28 °C

- **Time**: 5 days

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Tart-salty preserved plums, juice, and a powdered skin seasoning.
