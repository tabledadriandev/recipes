# Beurre nantais

Notes: Beurre blanc with Muscadet and shallots; classic with fish and shellfish.
Salt %: 0.8
Shelf Life (days): 0
Storage: Hold warm
Temp °C: Hold 55–60
Time: 15–25 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Reduce Muscadet and shallots to a light syrup.

2) Off heat, whisk in cold butter gradually. Season with salt, lemon.

3) Hold 55–60 °C; strain if smoother texture desired.

### Notes

- Add a spoon of cream pre‑mounting for extra stability if needed.
