# Gelée d'Agar-Agar (Base) #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Molecular #Gel

**Preface:** A foundational hydrocolloid preparation, utilizing agar-agar to create a firm, heat-stable gel from a pure liquid base.• 2 g agar-agar powder (1% of total weight)

**Ingredients**

- **Base:**

  - 200 g vegetable or fruit juice (strained)

  - 2 g agar-agar powder (1% of total weight)

**Method**

1. Bring the strained juice to a rapid boil.

2. Whisk in the agar-agar powder until fully dispersed.

3. Maintain a rolling boil for precisely 10 seconds to ensure the agar is fully activated.

4. Pour the liquid into molds or onto trays.

5. Chill until the gel is completely firm (approximately 30 minutes).

6. Cut or shape the gel as desired for plating.

**Technical Notes:** Agar-agar requires a full boil to activate and sets rapidly at room temperature, making it ideal for precise shaping.

**Chef’s Intention:** A disciplined, textural component that provides a clean, concentrated flavour delivery.
