# Chocolate soil (crumb)

Notes: Cocoa‑almond crumb baked to sandy texture.
Salt %: 0.2
Shelf Life (days): 7
Storage: 0–20 °C
Temp °C: Bake 160–170
Time: 12–18 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Rub butter, sugar, flour, cocoa, almond meal to crumb.

2) Bake 160–170 °C to dry.

3) Cool; crumble; store airtight.

### Notes

- Add salt flakes for finish.
