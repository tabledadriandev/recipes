# Action Checklist for Table d’Adrian

**Equipment & Prep**:

- Build a stable chamber: insulated box + heat mat + thermostat + humidistat.

- Standardize brines and salts: 2%, 4%, 8–10%, 12% ready-reckoner.

**Pantry Start**:

- Start the pantry with: lacto-plums, lacto-carrots, lemon-verbena kombucha, perry vinegar, barley koji, yellow peaso, pea shoyu, yeast/mushroom garum, and black garlic.

**Service Uses**:

- Glaze vegetables with yeast garum butter.

- Finish sauces with peaso.

- Brighten oils with fruit vinegars.

- Use kombucha gels.

- Use black-garlic purée for contrast.

**Process & Safety**:

- Log every batch: date, salt %, temp, pH, aroma notes, defects, actions.

- Keep a kill switch: chill, pasteurize, or salt-up when flavor is right.
