# Sauce verte (herb)

Notes: Herb‑blended mayonnaise/aioli; parsley, chive, tarragon, capers, lemon.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 2
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Cold prep
Time: 15–25 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Make a light mayonnaise base.

2) Blend herbs, capers, lemon zest/juice to a paste.

3) Fold into mayo; adjust salt and acid.

### Notes

- For brighter green, blend part of oil with herbs just before folding.
