## Detailed Explanation

48
INGREDIENTSPectin NH, on the other hand, is a special kind of LM pectin that has been processed to make it thermally 
reversible, meaning that it can be melted and reset as needed. This is particularly useful for certain glazes. Pectin has many applications in pâtisserie, including as a thickener in jams, glazes, creams, and fillings.
   good to know: Always mix pectin with sugar before adding it to the other ingredients to ensure even 
distribution.
   purchasing tips: Most pectin found in regular supermarkets is either high methoxyl (for full-sugar jams 
or jellies) or low methoxyl (for low-sugar preparations). Yellow pectin and pectin NH can be purchased in specialty shops or online.
   storage tips: Store tightly sealed.
