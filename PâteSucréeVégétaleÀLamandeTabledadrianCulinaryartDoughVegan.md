# Pâte Sucrée Végétale à l'Amande #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Dough #Vegan

**Preface:** A refined, plant-based sweet dough, utilizing almond flour and rice starch to achieve a delicate, crumbly texture.• 0.5 tsp vanilla extract (optional)

**Ingredients**

- **Base:**

  - 170 g all-purpose flour

  - 100 g vegan butter (diced, cold)

  - 55 g powdered sugar

  - 45 ml unsweetened almond milk

  - 35 g cornstarch

  - 20 g almond flour

  - 0.25 tsp fine salt

  - 0.5 tsp vanilla extract (optional)

**Method**

1. Sift and whisk all dry ingredients (flour, sugars, cornstarch, almond flour, salt) to ensure aeration.

2. Incorporate the diced, cold vegan butter into the dry mixture until a coarse, sandy texture is achieved.

3. Introduce the almond milk and vanilla extract. Mix until the dough just comes together.

4. Form a disc, wrap tightly, and chill for a minimum of 1 hour.

5. Roll the dough to 3mm thickness, line the tart molds, and dock the base before baking.

**Technical Notes:** The use of cold butter and minimal liquid ensures a short, tender crumb structure.\
**Chef’s Intention:** A subtle sweetness and delicate texture that frames the purity of the fruit component.\
