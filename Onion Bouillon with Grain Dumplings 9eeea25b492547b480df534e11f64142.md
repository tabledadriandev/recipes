# Onion Bouillon with Grain Dumplings

Cook time min: 20
Course: Veg
Ingredients: Onion bouillon 600 mL. Mixed grains 150 g. Koji 30 g. Chive oil 10 mL.
Method: Blend grains + koji. Shape ~20 g and poach 3 min. Serve in hot bouillon.
Prep time min: 20
Status: Approved
Technique: Poach dumplings
Yield g: 700
