# Beurre monté — citrus finish

Notes: Finish with lemon or yuzu for fish and veg.
Salt %: 0.8
Shelf Life (days): 2
Storage: Hold warm or chill rapid
Temp °C: 58–62 hold
Time: 10–15 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1. Simmer a small splash of water with a pinch of salt; off heat.
2. Whisk in cold butter cubes gradually to a satin emulsion.
3. Hold 58–62 °C; finish with citrus juice to taste.

### Notes

- If it tightens, whisk in a teaspoon of warm water.
