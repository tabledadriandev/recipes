# Service Applications (Table d’Adrian)

## Plating Ideas

- Brush **Lacto Cep Water** on roasted brassicas, finish with **Cardamom Oil**.

- Glaze beets with **Lacto Plum Juice** reduced to a light syrup; accent with **Toasted Hay Oil** aroma.

- Pair **Sumac Kombucha** with raw kohlrabi slices for acid-spark; add drops of **Árbol Oil**.

## Components Used

- Lacto Cep Water

- Cardamom Oil

- Lacto Plum Juice

- Toasted Hay Oil (infused from Toasted Hay)

- Sumac Kombucha

- Árbol Oil
