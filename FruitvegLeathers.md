# Fruit/Veg Leathers

## Technique

Purée fruit/vegetable → add 2–3% glucose + 0.2% citric acid → dehydrate at 55 °C for 6–10 h on silicone.

## Parameters

- **Glucose %**: 2–3%

- **Citric Acid %**: 0.2%

- **Temperature**: 55 °C

- **Time**: 6–10 h

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Flexible, concentrated flavor sheets.
