# Fumet (Fish Stock) Reduction

## Technique

Sweat fish bones 5 min (no color) + white wine + aromatics → simmer 20 min max → strain → reduce 30–50% → mount with butter or oil.

## Parameters

- **Simmer Time**: 20 min max

- **Reduction**: 30–50%

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Light, clean, concentrated fish stock.
