# Celery “Millefeuille,” Apple, Lovage

## Components

- Thin celery sheets (steamed)

- Lovage emulsion

- Green apple batons

- Celery leaf powder

## Method

- Steam celery sheets, layer with emulsion.

- Chill and slice.

- Serve cool with apple acidity.

## Parameters

- **Service**: Cool

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Crisp-laminar bite.

- **Aroma**: Chlorophyll aroma.

## Creative Pairing

A highly architectural dish where thin, steamed celery sheets are layered with a bright lovage emulsion, providing a crisp, herbaceous, and laminar bite.
