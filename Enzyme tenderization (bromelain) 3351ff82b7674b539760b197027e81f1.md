# Enzyme tenderization (bromelain)

Applications: Tough cuts, octopus
Confidence: 65
Corrections: Shorten contact, lower temp
Failure modes: Mealy texture
Medium: Water
Notes: Dose 0.05–0.2% carefully.
Sensory cue: Tender without mush
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 4–10
Time range: 15–90 min
Tools used: Scale, fridge
Type: Extract

### Method

1) Dissolve bromelain to 0.05–0.2% in cold water. 2) Apply to product at 4–10 °C for 15–90 min. 3) Rinse; cook or chill.

### Notes

- Sensory: tender without mush; avoid over‑tenderization.
- Test: dose and time; acceptance = tender texture, intact structure.
- Uses: tough cuts, octopus.
