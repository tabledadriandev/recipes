# Court bouillon (fish)

Notes: Aromatic base for poaching fish and shellfish. Acid 0.2–0.5%.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: 85–92 (simmer)
Time: 30–40 min
Type: Stock

### Method

1. Combine water, 1% salt, aromatics, and acid 0.2–0.5%.
2. Simmer 85–92 °C for 30–40 minutes; strain.
3. Use for fish and shellfish poaching; cool quickly to store.

### Notes

- Adjust acid level to protein and use-case.
