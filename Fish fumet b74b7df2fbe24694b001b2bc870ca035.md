# Fish fumet

Notes: Use white fish bones, no fatty fish.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 2
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: 82–90 (no boil)
Time: 30–45 min
Type: Stock

### Method

1. Sweat mirepoix in butter without color; add white fish bones.
2. Add white wine; reduce by half; add cold water and aromatics.
3. Simmer gently 30–45 min; strain without pressing; chill.

### Notes

- Keep below boil to avoid clouding.
