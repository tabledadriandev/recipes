# Juice clarification (freeze-thaw)

Applications: Clear fruit gels, broths
Confidence: 80
Corrections: Slower thaw, finer filter, antioxidant
Failure modes: Haze, oxidation
Medium: Water
Notes: Suspend filter over catch vessel.
Sensory cue: Clear drip, clean flavor
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): -18 freeze; 2–4 thaw
Time range: 12–36 h
Tools used: Freezer, sieve
Type: Clarify

### Method

1) Freeze strained juice at −18 °C until solid. 2) Thaw 2–4 °C through a fine filter over 12–36 h. 3) Collect clear drip; discard pulp.

### Notes

- Sensory: water‑clear drip, fresh aroma.
- Test: pre‑filter mesh and thaw time; acceptance = clear NTU, ≥75% yield.
- Uses: tomato water, clarified fruit bases, raw dressings.
