# Brown veal stock (fond brun)

Notes: Roasted bones with mirepoix and tomato paste; long gentle simmer; no salt.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 5
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Roast 200; simmer 90–95
Time: 8–12 h
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Roast veal bones at 200 °C to deep brown; add tomato paste for last 10–15 min.

2) Transfer to pot; deglaze trays; cover with cold water.

3) Simmer gently 8–12 h, skimming; add roasted mirepoix mid‑way. Strain fine; chill.

### Notes

- Clarify for a clear base if needed; salt only in derivative sauces.
