# Braised leeks in beurre monté

Notes: Gentle poach in beurre monté; finish with lemon.
Salt %: 1
Shelf Life (days): 2
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: 58–62 hold
Time: 25–35 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1. Trim and wash leeks; cut into logs.
2. In a pan, make beurre monté and submerge leeks halfway.
3. Cover and hold 58–62 °C until tender 25–35 min.
4. Glaze with pan juices; finish with lemon and salt.

### Notes

- Avoid boiling to keep emulsion intact.
