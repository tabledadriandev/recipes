# Pigeon with Juniper Jus

Cook time min: 25
Course: Meat
Ingredients: Pigeon 2. Butter 40 g. Shallot 50 g. Red wine 200 mL. Brown stock 300 mL. Juniper 2 g.
Method: Sear. Roast 200 °C to 52 °C. Deglaze wine, reduce. Add stock, finish butter + juniper.
Prep time min: 15
Status: Approved
Technique: Roast + pan sauce
Yield g: 500
