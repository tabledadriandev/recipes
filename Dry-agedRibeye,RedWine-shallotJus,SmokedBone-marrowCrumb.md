# Dry-Aged Ribeye, Red Wine-Shallot Jus, Smoked Bone-Marrow Crumb

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Ribeye 30–45 d dry-aged. Temper 45 min.

- **Cook**: Hard sear; finish to **56 °C core**.

- **Rest**: 10 min.

## Sauce & Finish

- **Sauce**: Red wine + shallot reduction → add demi-glace → reduce to nappé; finish with verjus drop (Jus Court technique).

- **Crumb**: Smoked bone-marrow crumb (panko + marrow fat + parsley + lemon zest) 2 min toast.

## Parameters

- **Dry Age**: 30–45 d

- **Core Temp**: 56 °C

- **Rest Time**: 10 min

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Crust crackles, center rosy.

- **Sauce**: Sauce shiny.
