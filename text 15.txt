Tahini Tuiles (Honeycomb Mold, 150°C)

Yields: ~12–20 tuiles (depending on mold size)

Ingredients:
	•	40 g egg whites (approx. 1 large egg white)
	•	35 g tahini (runny, well-mixed)
	•	30 g powdered sugar (or very fine caster)
	•	10 g rice flour (or all-purpose if not gluten-free)
	•	1 pinch fine salt
	•	Optional: a few drops of vanilla or sesame oil for aroma
