# Pickled shallots (quick)

Notes: Hot brine pour‑over for garnish acidity and crunch.
Salt %: 1.5
Shelf Life (days): 10
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Brine 95–100; hold 2–4
Time: 2–24 h
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Bring brine (3–5% acid, 1.5% salt) to a boil with aromatics.

2) Pour over sliced shallots; cool; refrigerate.

3) Use after 2–24 h.

### Notes

- Keep slices even for texture; consider rice vinegar for softer profile.
