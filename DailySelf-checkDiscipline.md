# Daily Self-Check Discipline

## Before Service

- Reheat sauces, skim properly, taste each.

- Smell every fridge.

- Check mise en place trays are dry, not wet or oxidized.

- Sharpen knives.

- Calibrate digital scale and thermometer.

- Review one random cost sheet with sous-chef.

- **Action**: Write one thing to fix tomorrow.
