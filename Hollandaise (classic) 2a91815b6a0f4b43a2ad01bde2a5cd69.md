# Hollandaise (classic)

Notes: Yolk sabayon mounted with clarified butter; lemon to taste.
Salt %: 0.8
Shelf Life (days): 0
Storage: Hold warm
Temp °C: Hold 57–60
Time: 15–25 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Whisk yolks with reduction over bain‑marie to ribbon.

2) Stream in warm clarified butter.

3) Season with lemon and salt; hold 57–60 °C.

### Notes

- Add a spoon of warm water to loosen if thickening.
