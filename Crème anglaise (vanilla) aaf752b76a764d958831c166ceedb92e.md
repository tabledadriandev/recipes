# Crème anglaise (vanilla)

Notes: Custard sauce base for desserts; do not boil.
Salt %: 0.1
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Nap 82–85
Time: 15–20 min
Type: Gel

### Method

1) Heat milk/cream with split vanilla to steaming.

2) Temper into yolks and sugar; cook to 82–85 °C nappe.

3) Strain; chill rapidly; hold cold.

### Notes

- Over 85 °C risks curdle; stir constantly and use a probe.
