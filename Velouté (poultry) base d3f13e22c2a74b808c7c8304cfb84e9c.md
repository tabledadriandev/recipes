# Velouté (poultry) base

Notes: White roux + white stock; mother sauce base.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 85–92
Time: 45–60 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Cook white roux (butter+flour) 3–5 min without color.

2) Whisk in hot white chicken stock gradually.

3) Simmer 30–45 min; skim; strain; adjust salt.

### Notes

- Derivatives: suprême (cream finish), sauce vin blanc (fish velouté + wine).
