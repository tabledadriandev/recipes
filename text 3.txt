Gelification


3. Basic Gel Recipe (Using Agar-Agar)

Ingredients:
	•	200 g vegetable or fruit juice
	•	2 g agar-agar powder (1% of total weight)

Method:
	1.	Bring juice to a boil.
	2.	Add agar-agar while whisking.
	3.	Boil 10 seconds.
	4.	Pour into molds or trays.
	5.	Chill until firm (~30 minutes in fridge).
	6.	Cut or shape as desired.

Substitutions:
	•	Parsley, carrot, beetroot, blueberry, basil infusion, even tomato water — identical proportions.
