# Mushroom jus (essence)

Notes: Roasted mushrooms deglazed and reduced; intense umami glaze.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 85–92; reduce 90–95
Time: 60–90 min
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Roast mushrooms and trimmings to deep brown.

2) Deglaze tray with water/dashi; simmer 45–60 min.

3) Strain; reduce to light glaze; mount with butter.

### Notes

- A dash of soy intensifies umami; add at finish.
