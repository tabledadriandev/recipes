# Mornay sauce

Notes: Cheese‑enriched béchamel; gratin base.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Simmer 85–92
Time: 20–30 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Make béchamel (white roux + milk) to nappe.

2) Off heat, whisk in grated Gruyère and Parmesan until smooth.

3) Season with white pepper and nutmeg.

### Notes

- Avoid boil after cheese; can finish with a little cream for gloss.
