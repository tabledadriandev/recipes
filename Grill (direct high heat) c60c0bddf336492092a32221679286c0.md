# Grill (direct high heat)

Applications: Veg, meats, fish finishing
Confidence: 80
Corrections: Dry surface, control fat drip, preheat longer
Failure modes: Flare-ups, underchar
Medium: Air
Notes: Resting essential.
Sensory cue: Defined grill marks, Maillard aroma
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 250–320 grate
Time range: 2–12 min
Tools used: Grill, probe
Type: Grill

### Method

1) Preheat grill to 250–320 °C; dry surface of product. 2) Grill direct heat to define marks; manage flare‑ups; finish to target core.

### Notes

- Sensory: defined marks, Maillard aroma; no acrid soot.
- Test: grate temp and preheat time; acceptance = even char without flare scorch.
- Uses: veg, meats, fish finishing.
