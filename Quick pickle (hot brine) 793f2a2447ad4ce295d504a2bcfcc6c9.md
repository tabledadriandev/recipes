# Quick pickle (hot brine)

Applications: Garnishes, relishes
Confidence: 85
Corrections: Reduce blanching, increase calcium, cooler storage
Failure modes: Mushy texture
Medium: Acid
Notes: Base: 3–5% acid, 1–2% salt.
Sensory cue: Bright acidity, firm bite
Source: EMP
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): Brine boil; veg 2–4
Time range: 2 h–7 d
Tools used: Saucepan, jars
Type: Cure

### Method

1) Bring brine (3–5% acid, 1–2% salt) to a boil. 2) Pack veg; pour hot brine; cool; hold 2–4 °C for 2 h–7 d.

### Notes

- Sensory: bright acidity, firm bite. Avoid mush by limiting blanching and using calcium if needed.
- Test: acid % and time; acceptance = crisp texture.
- Uses: garnishes, relishes.
