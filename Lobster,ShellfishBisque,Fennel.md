# Lobster, Shellfish Bisque, Fennel

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Parboil, shell; roast shells for bisque.

- **Cook**: Poach tails **54–56 °C butter**.

## Sauce & Finish

- **Sauce**: Bisque reduced to nappé.

- **Finish**: Fennel pollen.

## Parameters

- **Poach Temp**: 54–56 °C

## Plating Cues

- **Viscosity**: Bisque nappé.
