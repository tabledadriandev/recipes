# Green peppercorn sauce

Notes: Classic steak sauce with demi‑glace and cream finish.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 3
Storage: 0–3 °C
Temp °C: Reduce 90–95
Time: 30–45 min
Type: Jus

### Method

1) Sweat crushed green peppercorns and shallot in butter.

2) Deglaze with cognac; reduce by half.

3) Add demi‑glace; simmer to coat. Finish with a splash of cream and butter.

### Notes

- Adjust heat with peppercorn quantity; finish salt at end.
