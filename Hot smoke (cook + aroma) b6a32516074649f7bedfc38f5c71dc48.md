# Hot smoke (cook + aroma)

Applications: Fish, poultry, roots
Confidence: 80
Corrections: Lower temp, add humidity, brine
Failure modes: Dryness, acrid notes
Medium: Air
Notes: Monitor internal temp.
Sensory cue: Even color, cooked to target temp
Source: In-house
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 80–120
Time range: 30–180 min
Tools used: Smoker, probe
Type: Smoke

### Method

1) Smoke at 80–120 °C with humidity as needed; monitor core temperature. 2) Cook to doneness; rest before service.

### Notes

- Sensory: even color, gentle smoke; avoid dryness. Brine or baste to protect.
- Test: chamber temp and humidity; acceptance = tender, no acrid notes.
- Uses: fish, poultry, roots.
