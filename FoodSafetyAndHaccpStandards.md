# Food Safety and HACCP Standards

## Cold Chain

- Work 0–5 °C cold chain.

## Cross-Contamination

- Raw poultry and crustaceans must be handled on separate boards.

## Cooling Hot Stocks

- Cool hot stocks from 60 °C to 10 °C within 2 h (use shallow pans or an ice bath).

## Reheating Sauces

- Reheat sauces to **≥75 °C** once.

- Do not double-heat delicate emulsions.

## Labeling

- Label with date, time, cook temp, and chill complete status.
