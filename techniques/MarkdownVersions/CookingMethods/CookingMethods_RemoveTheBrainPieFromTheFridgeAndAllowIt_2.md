## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Remove the brain pie from the fridge and allow it to temper for 30 minutes before service

2. Heat 
the grapeseed oil in a pot to 200°C and fry the milk skin while basting it with hot oil for 25 to 30 seconds or until browned

3. Remove the pie and allow it drain on both sides over C -fold towels

4. Pipe truffle  purée onto the center of the nasturtium leaf, stem -side up, about 1.5 cm from the 
edge

5. Place the fried pie in and fold the nasturtium leaf over both sides

6. Place it in a napkin -lined 
basket and serve it immediately
