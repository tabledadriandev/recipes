## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. 18 g Söl Oil*  
50 g cold butter, grated on a cheese grater  
Mix the dry ingredients together well and then cut in the cold butter until the tart dough comes 
together, but don’t overwork it

2. Then add the söl oil and seaweed paste and mix again until fully 
incorporated

3. Place the dough into a covered container and allow it to rest in the fridge for 20 minutes

4. Portion the dough into manageable chunks and place it onto a Silpat

5. Using 2 single strips of Silpats placed onto the long side of the Silpat (as guides) place the dough in the  center
and cover it with a lightly oiled sheet of parchment paper and proceed to roll out the tart doughinto a thin sheet

6. Once you have your sheet rolled out, use an 8 cm ring mold to punch out as many portions as possible, and transfer the sheet to the  blast freezer
