## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. bottom side, rotate the æbleskiver 90 degrees, allowing the raw batter in the middle to spill 
back into the semicircle of the machine

2. Your æbleskiver in progress should resemble a sphere 
with one quarter empty

3. Allow the æbleskiver to set and caramelize again

4. Rotate the æbleskiver 90 degrees toward you 
again, allowing the raw batter in the middle to spill back into the semicircle of the machine

5. Allow the batter to set and begin to caramelize again

6. Rotate the æbleskiver again, 90 degrees
up from the side that is flush with the semicircle mold of the machine

7. From the top down, thereshould be a small hole in the top of your æbleskiver

8. Pipe in a bit more batter to fill this hole andflip the entire æbleskiver upside down so the hole is now touching the heating element of the 
æbleskiver  machine

9. Add more bear fat and continue to cook and caramelize

10. Do not be afraid to add additional bearfat as necessary
