# Sift the flour with the baking powder and beat into the mixture.

**Category:** Knife Skills  
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

---

## Overview

287
Sift the flour with the baking powder and beat into the mixture. 
Preheat the oven to 400°F  (200°C /Gas mark 6 ).
Using the tip of a knife or a toothpick, marble the two mixtures 
together by making zigzag patterns. Bake for 15 minutes and  
then lower the oven temperature to 325°F  (160°C /Gas mark 3 ) 
and bake for an additional 20–25  minutes.2 •
