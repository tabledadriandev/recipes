# Roasted Cauliflower “Steak,” Almond–Caper Sauce Grenobloise

## Components

- Roasted cauliflower

- Almond butter-browned oil

- Caper-lemon–raisin relish

- Parsley

## Method

- Sear, roast cauliflower.

- Spoon hot “grenobloise” (almond-caper sauce).

- Parsley rain.

## Parameters

- **Sauce Base**: Almond butter-browned oil (Brown Butter Notes technique)

## Plating Cues

- **Service**: Sizzling service.

- **Flavor**: Sweet–salty–acidic.

## Creative Pairing

A plant-based "steak" preparation where roasted cauliflower is finished with a hot, savory, and acidic almond-caper sauce, mimicking the classic _grenobloise_ flavor profile.
