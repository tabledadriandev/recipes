# Herb-Brioche Crumble, Celeriac Cream, Black Truffle

## Components

- Herb crumble (oil-bound)

- Whipped celeriac cream

- Shaved truffle

## Method

- Bake crumble to sandy texture.

- Pipe cream.

- Shave truffle at the pass.

## Parameters

- **Crumble Prep**: Oil-bound, baked to sandy texture

## Plating Cues

- **Contrast**: Warm crumb vs cool cream.

- **Aroma**: Volatile truffle.

## Creative Pairing

A textural and temperature contrast where warm, aromatic herb crumble meets cool, silken celeriac cream, finished with the volatile perfume of shaved black truffle.
