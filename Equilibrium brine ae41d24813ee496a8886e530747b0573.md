# Equilibrium brine

Applications: Poultry, pork, veg
Confidence: 85
Corrections: Lower % or time, add calcium for veg
Failure modes: Oversalt, mushy
Medium: Water
Notes: 2–3% meat brine. 1–2% veg brine.
Sensory cue: Even seasoning, improved juiciness
Source: EMP
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 0–4
Time range: 2–24 h
Tools used: Scale, container
Type: Cure

**Principle**

Diffusion-based brining to equilibrate seasoning and improve juiciness.

- Parameters
    - Meat brine: 2–3% salt
    - Veg brine: 1–2% salt
    - Temp: 0–4 °C
    - Time: 2–24 h by thickness
- Goal
    - Texture: moist, seasoned throughout
    - Aroma: clean, no cure harshness

<aside>
⚠️

Sensory benchmark: even seasoning without surface salinity

</aside>

- Test
    - Variable: % salt vs time by cut thickness
    - Observe: % weight gain, salinity profile
    - Acceptance: target % gain without surface salt burn
- Uses
    - Veg: carrots pre-roast
    - Meat: poultry crowns
    - Fish: cod loins
    - Dessert: saline syrups for fruit balance
