# Tomato Two Ways: Chilled Consommé & Warm Confit

## Components

- Agar-clarified consommé

- Low-temp confit wedges in tomato oil

- Basil seed gel pearls

## Method

- Split hot/cold in bowl.

- Finish with basil oil.

## Parameters

- **Consommé**: Agar-clarified

- **Confit**: Low-temp in tomato oil

## Plating Cues

- **Contrast**: Maximal tomato identity, temperature as contrast.

## Creative Pairing

A dish that maximizes the flavor of tomato by contrasting a cold, clear, acidic consommé with warm, rich, low-temperature confit wedges.
