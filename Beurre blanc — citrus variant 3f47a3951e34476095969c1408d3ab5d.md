# Beurre blanc — citrus variant

Notes: Add citrus juices/zest to shallot reduction; mount with cold butter.
Salt %: 0.8
Shelf Life (days): 0
Storage: Hold warm
Temp °C: Hold 55–60
Time: 15–25 min
Type: Emulsion

### Method

1) Reduce shallot with white wine, splash of citrus juice to light syrup.

2) Off heat, whisk in cold butter gradually; strain.

3) Finish with zest and a drop of juice; hold 55–60 °C.

### Notes

- Balance acid with a touch of cream if needed before mounting.
