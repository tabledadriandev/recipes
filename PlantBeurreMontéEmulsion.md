# Plant Beurre Monté Emulsion

## Technique

Combine 3 parts aromatic stock + 1 part neutral oil → shear in 0.2–0.3% xanthan (or 0.5% sunflower lecithin) warm to 65 °C.

## Parameters

- **Ratio**: 3 parts stock : 1 part oil

- **Xanthan %**: 0.2–0.3% (or 0.5% sunflower lecithin)

- **Temperature**: 65 °C (warm shear)

## Sensory Cues

- **Result**: Stable, dairy-free beurre monté.
