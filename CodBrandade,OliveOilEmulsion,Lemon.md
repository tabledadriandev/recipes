# Cod Brandade, Olive Oil Emulsion, Lemon

## Prep & Cook

- **Prep**: Cure cod 2% salt 30 min; poach in milk; flake.

- **Mix**: Olive oil emulsion + garlic + potato; lemon to lift.

## Finish

- Quenelle with herb salad.

## Parameters

- **Cure Salt**: 2%

- **Cure Time**: 30 min

## Plating Cues

- **Texture**: Light, emulsified brandade.
