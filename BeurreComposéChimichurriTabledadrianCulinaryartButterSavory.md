# Beurre Composé Chimichurri #TabledAdrian #CulinaryArt #Butter #Savory

**Preface:** A vibrant, aromatic compound butter, capturing the fresh, herbaceous intensity of the Mediterranean garden.

**Ingredients**

- **Herb Blend:**

  - 500 g unsalted butter (melted)

  - 100 g Ligurian olive oil

  - 10 g mint leaves

  - 10 g flat-leaf parsley

  - 10 g coriander

  - 10 g basil leaves

  - 5 g thyme

  - 5 g rosemary

  - 2 cloves garlic

  - Juice of 1 lemon

  - Salt, black pepper (to season)

- **Vegan Alternative:** Plant-based butter substitute

**Method**

1. Combine all herbs, garlic, lemon juice, and olive oil. Blend until the herbs are finely chopped and the mixture is a vibrant green purée.

2. Incorporate the herb purée into the melted butter. Mix until homogenous.

3. Pour the mixture into a mold or onto parchment paper. Form into a disciplined log and chill until firm.

4. For the vegan alternative, substitute the unsalted butter with a plant-based alternative and follow the same method.

**Technical Notes:** The use of melted butter ensures a uniform distribution of the herb purée before setting.

**Chef’s Intention:** A disciplined, aromatic accent that provides a fresh, acidic counterpoint to grilled proteins.
