# Morel Mushroom Au Poivre

Cook time min: 15
Course: Veg
Ingredients: Morels 300 g. Green peppercorn 15 g. Cashew cream 80 mL. Brandy 30 mL. Mushroom jus 150 mL.
Method: Char morels. Deglaze brandy. Add jus + peppercorns. Finish cream + chives.
Prep time min: 15
Status: Approved
Technique: Pan glaze
Yield g: 450
