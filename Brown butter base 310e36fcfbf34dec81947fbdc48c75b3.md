# Brown butter base

Notes: Clarify through filter before storage.
Salt %: 0
Shelf Life (days): 7
Storage: Chilled 0–3 °C
Temp °C: 145–160
Time: 5–8 min
Type: Oil

### Method

1. Melt butter over medium heat until fully liquid.
2. Increase heat to medium-high; simmer until milk solids turn golden.
3. Reduce heat and continue until amber with hazelnut aroma.
4. Immediately remove from heat; strain through fine mesh.
5. Chill rapidly over ice for storage.

### Notes

- Target peak 150–155 °C for nutty aroma.
- Stop before solids blacken to avoid bitterness.
