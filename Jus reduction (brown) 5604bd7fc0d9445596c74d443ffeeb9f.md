# Jus reduction (brown)

Applications: Sauces, glazes
Confidence: 90
Corrections: Lower heat, skim often, clarify
Failure modes: Scorching, muddy flavor
Medium: Water
Notes: Salt at end only.
Sensory cue: Nappe, glossy, no bitterness
Source: EMP
Standardized: No
Temperature range (°C): 90–98
Time range: 2–6 h
Tools used: Stockpot, fine chinois
Type: Reduction

### Method

1) Reduce brown stock at 90–98 °C, skimming often. 2) Clarify if needed. 3) Finish to nappe; season only at end.

### Notes

- Sensory: glossy, nappe, no bitterness. Salt only after reduction.
- Test: reduction time and skimming frequency; acceptance = clean flavor, shine.
- Uses: sauces, glazes.
